HETAY ECLARATIONDAY FOAY NDEPENDENCEIAY FOAY HETAY NITEDUAY TATESSAY FOAY MERICAAAY 
 
 
Enwhay inyay ethay Oursecay ofyay umanhay eventsyay, ityay ecomesbay ecessarynay orfay 
oneyay eoplepay otay issolveday ethay oliticalpay andsbay ichwhay avehay onnectedcay 
emthay ithway anotheryay, andyay otay assumeyay, amongyay ethay Owerspay ofyay ethay earthyay, 
ethay eparatesay andyay equalyay ationstay otay ichwhay ethay Awslay ofyay Aturenay andyay 
ofyay Ature'snay Odgay entitleyay emthay, ayay ecentday espectray otay ethay opinionsyay 
ofyay ankindmay equiresray atthay eythay ouldshay eclareday ethay ausescay ichwhay 
impelyay emthay otay ethay eparationsay. 
 
Eway oldhay esethay uthstray otay ebay elf-evidentsay, atthay allyay enmay areyay eatedcray equalyay, 
atthay eythay areyay endowedyay ybay eirthay Eatorcray ithway ertaincay unalienableyay Ightsray, 
atthay amongyay esethay areyay Ifelay, Ibertylay, andyay ethay ursuitpay ofyay Appinesshay. 
Atthay otay ecuresay esethay ightsray, Overnmentsgay areyay institutedyay amongyay Enmay, 
erivingday eirthay ustjay owerspay omfray ethay onsentcay ofyay ethay overnedgay, 
Atthay eneverwhay anyyay Ormfay ofyay Overnmentgay ecomesbay estructiveday ofyay esethay endsyay, 
ityay isyay ethay Ightray ofyay ethay Eoplepay otay alteryay oryay otay abolishyay ityay, andyay otay instituteyay 
ewnay Overnmentgay, ayinglay itsyay oundationfay onyay uchsay inciplespray andyay organizingyay 
itsyay owerspay inyay uchsay ormfay, asyay otay emthay allshay eemsay ostmay ikelylay otay effectyay 
eirthay Afetysay andyay Appinesshay.  Udencepray, indeedyay, illway ictateday atthay Overnmentsgay 
onglay establishedyay ouldshay otnay ebay angedchay orfay ightlay andyay ansienttray ausescay; 
andyay accordinglyyay allyay experienceyay athhay ownshay, atthay ankindmay areyay oremay isposedday 
otay uffersay, ilewhay evilsyay areyay ufferablesay, anthay otay ightray emselvesthay ybay abolishingyay 
ethay ormsfay otay ichwhay eythay areyay accustomedyay.  Utbay enwhay ayay onglay aintray ofyay abusesyay andyay 
usurpationsyay, ursuingpay invariablyyay ethay amesay Objectyay evincesyay ayay esignday otay educeray 
emthay underyay absoluteyay Espotismday, ityay isyay eirthay ightray, ityay isyay eirthay utyday, otay owthray 
offyay uchsay Overnmentgay, andyay otay ovidepray ewnay Uardsgay orfay eirthay uturefay ecuritysay. 
uch--Say ashay eenbay ethay atientpay ufferancesay ofyay esethay Oloniescay; andyay uchsay isyay ownay 
ethay ecessitynay ichwhay onstrainscay emthay otay alteryay eirthay ormerfay Ystemssay ofyay Overnmentgay. 
Ethay istoryhay ofyay ethay esentpray Ingkay ofyay Eatgray Itainbray isyay ayay istoryhay ofyay epeatedray 
injuriesyay andyay usurpationsyay, allyay avinghay inyay irectday objectyay ethay establishmentyay 
ofyay anyay absoluteyay Yrannytay overyay esethay Atesstay.  Otay ovepray isthay, etlay Actsfay 
ebay ubmittedsay otay ayay andidcay orldway. 
 
Ehay ashay efusedray ishay Assentyay otay Awslay, ethay ostmay olesomewhay andyay ecessarynay 
orfay ethay ublicpay oodgay. 
 
Ehay ashay orbiddenfay ishay Overnorsgay otay asspay Awslay ofyay immediateyay 
andyay essingpray importanceyay, unlessyay uspendedsay inyay eirthay operationyay 
illtay ishay Assentyay ouldshay ebay obtainedyay; andyay enwhay osay uspendedsay, 
ehay ashay utterlyyay eglectednay otay attendyay otay emthay. 
 
Ehay ashay efusedray otay asspay otheryay Awslay orfay ethay accommodationyay ofyay 
argelay istrictsday ofyay eoplepay, unlessyay osethay eoplepay ouldway elinquishray 
ethay ightray ofyay Epresentationray inyay ethay Egislaturelay, ayay ightray 
inestimableyay otay emthay andyay ormidablefay otay yrantstay onlyyay. 
 
Ehay ashay alledcay ogethertay egislativelay odiesbay atyay acesplay unusualyay, 
uncomfortableyay, andyay istantday omfray ethay epositoryday ofyay eirthay 
Ublicpay Ecordsray, orfay ethay olesay urposepay ofyay atiguingfay emthay 
intoyay ompliancecay ithway ishay easuresmay. 
 
Ehay ashay issolvedday Epresentativeray Ouseshay epeatedlyray, orfay opposingyay 
ithway anlymay irmnessfay ishay invasionsyay onyay ethay ightsray ofyay ethay eoplepay. 
 
Ehay ashay efusedray orfay ayay onglay imetay, afteryay uchsay issolutionsday, 
otay ausecay othersyay otay ebay electedyay; erebywhay ethay Egislativelay Owerspay, 
incapableyay ofyay Annihilationyay, avehay eturnedray otay ethay Eoplepay atyay argelay 
orfay eirthay exerciseyay; ethay Atestay emainingray inyay ethay eanmay imetay exposedyay 
otay allyay ethay angersday ofyay invasionyay omfray ithoutway, andyay onvulsionscay ithinway. 
 
Ehay ashay endeavouredyay otay eventpray ethay opulationpay ofyay esethay Atesstay; 
orfay atthay urposepay obstructingyay ethay Awslay ofyay Aturalizationnay ofyay Oreignersfay; 
efusingray otay asspay othersyay otay encourageyay eirthay igrationmay itherhay, 
andyay aisingray ethay onditionscay ofyay ewnay Appropriationsyay ofyay Andslay. 
 
Ehay ashay obstructedyay ethay Administrationyay ofyay Usticejay, ybay efusingray ishay Assentyay 
otay Awslay orfay establishingyay Udiciaryjay Owerspay. 
 
Ehay ashay ademay udgesjay ependentday onyay ishay Illway aloneyay, orfay ethay enuretay 
ofyay eirthay officesyay, andyay ethay amountyay andyay aymentpay ofyay eirthay alariessay. 
 
Ehay ashay erectedyay ayay ultitudemay ofyay Ewnay Officesyay, andyay entsay itherhay armssway ofyay 
Officersyay otay arasshay ouryay Eoplepay, andyay eatyay outyay eirthay ubstancesay. 
 
Ehay ashay eptkay amongyay usyay, inyay imestay ofyay eacepay, Andingstay Armiesyay 
ithoutway ethay Onsentcay ofyay ouryay egislatureslay. 
 
Ehay ashay affectedyay otay enderray ethay Ilitarymay independentyay ofyay 
andyay uperiorsay otay ethay Ivilcay Owerpay. 
 
Ehay ashay ombinedcay ithway othersyay otay ubjectsay usyay otay ayay urisdictionjay 
oreignfay otay ouryay onstitutioncay, andyay unacknowledgedyay ybay ouryay awslay; 
ivinggay ishay Assentyay otay eirthay Actsyay ofyay etendedpray egislationlay: 
 
Orfay uarteringqay argelay odiesbay ofyay armedyay oopstray amongyay usyay: 
 
Orfay otectingpray emthay, ybay ayay ockmay Ialtray, omfray Unishmentpay orfay anyyay Urdersmay 
ichwhay eythay ouldshay ommitcay onyay ethay Inhabitantsyay ofyay esethay Atesstay: 
 
Orfay uttingcay offyay ouryay Adetray ithway allyay artspay ofyay ethay orldway: 
 
Orfay imposingyay axestay onyay usyay ithoutway ouryay Onsentcay: 
 
Orfay eprivingday usyay, inyay anymay asescay, ofyay ethay enefitsbay ofyay Ialtray ybay Uryjay: 
 
Orfay ansportingtray usyay eyondbay Eassay otay ebay iedtray orfay etendedpray offencesyay: 
 
Orfay abolishingyay ethay eefray Ystemsay ofyay Englishyay Awslay inyay ayay eighbouringnay 
Ovincepray, establishingyay ereinthay anyay Arbitraryyay overnmentgay, 
andyay enlargingyay itsyay Oundariesbay osay asyay otay enderray ityay atyay onceyay 
anyay exampleyay andyay itfay instrumentyay orfay introducingyay ethay amesay 
absoluteyay uleray intoyay esethay Oloniescay: 
 
Orfay akingtay awayyay ouryay Arterschay, abolishingyay ouryay ostmay aluablevay Awslay, 
andyay alteringyay undamentallyfay ethay Ormsfay ofyay ouryay Overnmentsgay: 
 
Orfay uspendingsay ouryay ownyay Egislatureslay, andyay eclaringday emselvesthay 
investedyay ithway Owerpay otay egislatelay orfay usyay inyay allyay asescay atsoeverwhay. 
 
Ehay ashay abdicatedyay Overnmentgay erehay, ybay eclaringday usyay outyay ofyay ishay Otectionpray 
andyay agingway Arway againstyay usyay. 
 
Ehay ashay underedplay ouryay eassay, avagedray ouryay Oastscay, urntbay ouryay ownstay, 
andyay estroyedday ethay iveslay ofyay ouryay eoplepay. 
 
Ehay isyay atyay isthay imetay ansportingtray argelay armiesyay ofyay oreignfay ercenariesmay 
otay ompleatcay ethay orksway ofyay eathday, esolationday andyay yrannytay, alreadyyay egunbay 
ithway ircumstancescay ofyay Ueltycray & erfidypay arcelyscay aralleledpay inyay ethay 
ostmay arbarousbay agesyay, andyay otallytay unworthyyay ofyay ethay Eadhay ofyay ayay ivilizedcay ationnay. 
 
Ehay ashay onstrainedcay ouryay ellowfay Itizenscay akentay Aptivecay onyay ethay ighhay Eassay 
otay earbay Armsyay againstyay eirthay Ountrycay, otay ecomebay ethay executionersyay ofyay 
eirthay iendsfray andyay Ethrenbray, oryay otay allfay emselvesthay ybay eirthay Andshay. 
 
Ehay ashay excitedyay omesticday insurrectionsyay amongstyay usyay, andyay ashay 
endeavouredyay otay ingbray onyay ethay inhabitantsyay ofyay ouryay ontiersfray, 
ethay ercilessmay Indianyay Avagessay, osewhay ownknay uleray ofyay arfareway, 
isyay anyay undistinguishedyay estructionday ofyay allyay agesyay, exessay andyay onditionscay. 
 
Inyay everyyay agestay ofyay esethay Oppressionsyay Eway avehay Etitionedpay orfay Edressray 
inyay ethay ostmay umblehay ermstay:  Ouryay epeatedray Etitionspay avehay eenbay answeredyay 
onlyyay ybay epeatedray injuryyay.  Ayay Incepray, osewhay aracterchay isyay usthay arkedmay 
ybay everyyay actyay ichwhay aymay efineday ayay Yranttay, isyay unfityay otay ebay ethay ulerray 
ofyay ayay eefray Eoplepay. 
 
Ornay avehay Eway eenbay antingway inyay attentionyay otay ouryay Itishbray ethrenbray. 
Eway avehay arnedway emthay omfray imetay otay imetay ofyay attemptsyay ybay eirthay 
egislaturelay otay extendyay anyay unwarrantableyay urisdictionjay overyay usyay. 
Eway avehay emindedray emthay ofyay ethay ircumstancescay ofyay ouryay emigrationyay andyay 
ettlementsay erehay.  Eway avehay appealedyay otay eirthay ativenay usticejay 
andyay agnanimitymay, andyay eway avehay onjuredcay emthay ybay ethay iestay ofyay ouryay

interruptyay ouryay onnectionscay andyay orrespondencecay.  Eythay ootay avehay eenbay 
eafday otay ethay oicevay ofyay usticejay andyay ofyay onsanguinitycay.  Eway ustmay, ereforethay, 
acquiesceyay inyay ethay ecessitynay, ichwhay enouncesday ouryay Eparationsay, andyay oldhay emthay, 
asyay eway oldhay ethay estray ofyay ankindmay, Enemiesyay inyay Arway, inyay Eacepay Iendsfray. 
 
Eway, ereforethay, ethay Epresentativesray ofyay ethay Unitedyay Atesstay ofyay Americayay,

andyay ofyay Ightray oughtyay otay ebay Eefray andyay Independentyay Atesstay;

Ethay Unitedyay Atesstay Illbay ofyay Ightsray. 
 
Ethay Entay Originalyay Amendmentsyay otay ethay Onstitutioncay ofyay ethay Unitedyay Atesstay

Iyay 
 
Ongresscay allshay akemay onay awlay espectingray anyay establishmentyay ofyay eligionray, 
oryay ohibitingpray ethay eefray exerciseyay ereofthay; oryay abridgingyay ethay eedomfray ofyay eechspay, 
oryay ofyay ethay esspray, oryay ethay ightray ofyay ethay eoplepay eaceablypay otay assembleyay, 
andyay otay etitionpay ethay Overnmentgay orfay ayay edressray ofyay ievancesgray. 
 
 
 
 
 
IIAY 
 
Ayay ell-regulatedway ilitiamay, eingbay ecessarynay otay ethay ecuritysay ofyay ayay eefray Atestay, 
ethay ightray ofyay ethay eoplepay otay eepkay andyay earbay armsyay, allshay otnay ebay infringedyay. 
 
 
 
 
IIIAY 
 
Onay oldiersay allshay, inyay imetay ofyay eacepay ebay uarteredqay inyay anyyay ousehay, 
ithoutway ethay onsentcay ofyay ethay owneryay, ornay inyay imetay ofyay arway, 
utbay inyay ayay annermay otay ebay escribedpray ybay awlay. 
 
 
 
 
VIAY 
 
Ethay ightray ofyay ethay eoplepay otay ebay ecuresay inyay eirthay ersonspay, ouseshay, aperspay, 
andyay effectsyay, againstyay unreasonableyay earchessay andyay eizuressay, allshay otnay ebay iolatedvay, 
andyay onay Arrantsway allshay issueyay, utbay uponyay obablepray ausecay, upportedsay ybay oathyay 
oryay affirmationyay, andyay articularlypay escribingday ethay aceplay otay ebay earchedsay, 
andyay ethay ersonspay oryay ingsthay otay ebay eizedsay. 
 
 
 
 
Vay 
 
Onay ersonpay allshay ebay eldhay otay answeryay orfay ayay apitalcay, oryay otherwiseyay infamousyay imecray, 
unlessyay onyay ayay esentmentpray oryay indictmentyay ofyay ayay Andgray Uryjay, exceptyay inyay asescay arisingyay 
inyay ethay andlay oryay avalnay orcesfay, oryay inyay ethay Ilitiamay, enwhay inyay actualyay ervicesay 
inyay imetay ofyay Arway oryay ublicpay angerday; ornay allshay anyyay ersonpay ebay ubjectsay orfay 
ethay amesay offenseyay otay ebay icetway utpay inyay eopardyjay ofyay ifelay oryay imblay; 
ornay allshay ebay ompelledcay inyay anyyay iminalcray asecay otay ebay ayay itnessway againstyay imselfhay, 
ornay ebay eprivedday ofyay ifelay, ibertylay, oryay opertypray, ithoutway ueday ocesspray ofyay awlay; 
ornay allshay ivatepray opertypray ebay akentay orfay ublicpay useyay ithoutway ustjay ompensationcay. 
 
 
 
 
IVAY 
 
Inyay allyay iminalcray osecutionspray, ethay accusedyay allshay enjoyyay ethay ightray otay ayay 
eedyspay andyay ublicpay ialtray, ybay anyay impartialyay uryjay ofyay ethay Atestay andyay istrictday 
ereinwhay ethay imecray allshay avehay eenbay ommittedcay, ichwhay istrictday allshay avehay 
eenbay eviouslypray ascertainedyay ybay awlay, andyay otay ebay informedyay ofyay ethay aturenay 
andyay ausecay ofyay ethay accusationyay; otay ebay onfrontedcay ithway ethay itnessesway againstyay imhay; 
otay avehay ompulsorycay ocesspray orfay obtainingyay itnessesway inyay ishay avorfay, 
andyay otay avehay ethay assistanceyay ofyay ounselcay orfay ishay efenseday. 
 
 
 
 
IIVAY 
 
Inyay uitssay atyay ommoncay awlay, erewhay ethay aluevay inyay ontroversycay allshay exceedyay 
entytway ollarsday, ethay ightray ofyay ialtray ybay uryjay allshay ebay eservedpray, 
andyay onay actfay iedtray ybay ayay uryjay allshay ebay otherwiseyay e-examinedray inyay anyyay ourtcay 
ofyay ethay Unitedyay Atesstay, anthay accordingyay otay ethay ulesray ofyay ethay ommoncay awlay. 
 
 
 
 
IIIVAY 
 
Excessiveyay ailbay allshay otnay ebay equiredray ornay excessiveyay inesfay imposedyay, 
ornay uelcray andyay unusualyay unishmentspay inflictedyay. 
 
 
 
 
XIAY 
 
Ethay enumerationyay inyay ethay Onstitutioncay, ofyay ertaincay ightsray, 
allshay otnay ebay onstruedcay otay enyday oryay isparageday othersyay etainedray ybay ethay eoplepay. 
 
 
 
 
Xay 
 
Ethay owerspay otnay elegatedday otay ethay Unitedyay Atesstay ybay ethay Onstitutioncay, 
ornay ohibitedpray ybay ityay otay ethay Atesstay, areyay eservedray otay ethay Atesstay espectivelyray, 
oryay otay ethay eoplepay.

HETAY ONSTITUTIONCAY FOAY HETAY NITEDUAY TATESSAY FOAY MERICAAAY, 1787 
 
 
 
Eway ethay eoplepay ofyay ethay Unitedyay Atesstay, inyay Orderyay otay ormfay ayay oremay erfectpay Unionyay, 
establishyay Usticejay, insureyay omesticday Anquilitytray, ovidepray orfay ethay ommoncay efenceday, 
omotepray ethay eneralgay Elfareway, andyay ecuresay ethay Essingsblay ofyay Ibertylay otay ourselvesyay 
andyay ouryay Osteritypay, oday ordainyay andyay establishyay isthay Onstitutioncay orfay ethay 
Unitedyay Atesstay ofyay Americayay. 
 
 
Articleyay 1 
 
Ectionsay 1.  Allyay egislativelay Owerspay ereinhay antedgray allshay ebay estedvay inyay ayay 
Ongresscay ofyay ethay Unitedyay Atesstay, ichwhay allshay onsistcay ofyay ayay Enatesay andyay 
Ousehay ofyay Epresentativesray. 
 
Ectionsay 2.  Ethay Ousehay ofyay Epresentativesray allshay ebay omposedcay ofyay Embersmay 
osenchay everyyay econdsay Earyay ybay ethay Eoplepay ofyay ethay everalsay Atesstay, 
andyay ethay electorsyay inyay eachyay Atestay allshay avehay ethay ualificationsqay equisiteray 
orfay electorsyay ofyay ethay ostmay umerousnay anchbray ofyay ethay Atestay egislaturelay. 
 
Onay Ersonpay allshay ebay ayay Epresentativeray owhay allshay otnay avehay attainedyay otay ethay 
Ageyay ofyay entytway ivefay Earsyay, andyay eenbay evensay Earsyay ayay itizencay ofyay ethay Unitedyay Atesstay, 
andyay owhay allshay otnay, enwhay electedyay, ebay anyay Inhabitantyay ofyay atthay Atestay inyay ichwhay 
ehay allshay ebay osenchay. 
 
Epresentativesray andyay irectday Axestay allshay ebay apportionedyay amongyay 
ethay everalsay Atesstay ichwhay aymay ebay includedyay ithinway isthay Unionyay, 
accordingyay otay eirthay espectiveray Umbersnay, ichwhay allshay ebay eterminedday 
ybay addingyay otay ethay olewhay umbernay ofyay eefray Ersonspay, includingyay osethay 
oundbay otay Ervicesay orfay ayay Ermtay ofyay Earsyay, andyay excludingyay Indiansyay otnay axedtay, 
eethray ifthsfay ofyay allyay otheryay Ersonspay.  Ethay actualyay Enumerationyay allshay ebay ademay 
ithinway eethray Earsyay afteryay ethay irstfay Eetingmay ofyay ethay Ongresscay ofyay ethay 
Unitedyay Atesstay, andyay ithinway everyyay ubsequentsay Ermtay ofyay entay Earsyay, 
inyay uchsay Annermay asyay eythay allshay ybay awlay Irectday.  Ethay umbernay ofyay 
Epresentativesray allshay otnay exceedyay oneyay orfay everyyay irtythay Ousandthay, 
utbay eachyay Atestay allshay avehay atyay eastlay oneyay Epresentativeray; 
andyay untilyay uchsay enumerationyay allshay ebay ademay, ethay Atestay ofyay Ewnay Ampshirehay 
allshay ebay entitledyay otay usechay eethray, Assachusettsmay eightyay, Oderhay Islandyay 
andyay Ovidencepray Antationsplay oneyay, Onnecticutcay ivefay, Ewnay Orkyay ixsay, 
Ewnay Erseyjay ourfay, Ennsylvaniapay eightyay, Elawareday oneyay, Arylandmay ixsay, 
Irginiavay entay, Orthnay Arolinacay ivefay, Outhsay Arolinacay ivefay, andyay Eorgiagay eethray. 
 
Enwhay acanciesvay appenhay inyay ethay Epresentationray omfray anyyay Atestay, ethay Executiveyay 
Authorityyay ereofthay allshay issueyay Itswray ofyay Electionyay otay illfay uchsay Acanciesvay. 
 
Ethay Ousehay ofyay Epresentativesray allshay usechay eirthay Eakerspay andyay otheryay Officersyay; 
andyay allshay avehay ethay olesay Owerpay ofyay Impeachmentyay. 
 
Ectionsay 3.  Ethay Enatesay ofyay ethay Unitedyay Atesstay allshay ebay omposedcay ofyay 
otway Enatorssay omfray eachyay Atestay, osenchay ybay ethay egislaturelay ereofthay, 
orfay ixsay Earsyay; andyay eachyay Enatorsay allshay avehay oneyay Otevay. 
 
Immediatelyyay afteryay eythay allshay ebay assembledyay inyay Onsequencecay ofyay ethay irstfay Electionyay, 
eythay allshay ebay ividedday asyay equallyyay asyay aymay ebay intoyay eethray Assesclay.  Ethay Eatssay ofyay 
ethay Enatorssay ofyay ethay irstfay Assclay allshay ebay acatedvay atyay ethay expirationyay ofyay ethay 
econdsay Earyay, ofyay ethay econdsay Assclay atyay ethay expirationyay ofyay ethay ourthfay Earyay, 
andyay ofyay ethay irdthay Assclay atyay ethay expirationyay ofyay ethay ixthsay Earyay, osay atthay oneyay irdthay 
aymay ebay osenchay everyyay econdsay Earyay; andyay ifyay acanciesvay appenhay ybay Esignationray, 
oryay otherwiseyay, uringday ethay ecessray ofyay ethay Egislaturelay ofyay anyyay Atestay, 
ethay Executiveyay ereofthay aymay akemay emporarytay Appointmentsyay untilyay ethay 
extnay eetingmay ofyay ethay Egislaturelay, ichwhay allshay enthay illfay uchsay Acanciesvay. 
 
Onay ersonpay allshay ebay ayay Enatorsay owhay allshay otnay avehay attainedyay otay ethay Ageyay ofyay 
irtythay Earsyay, andyay eenbay inenay Earsyay ayay Itizencay ofyay ethay Unitedyay Atesstay, 
andyay owhay allshay otnay, enwhay electedyay, ebay anyay Inhabitantyay ofyay atthay Atestay 
orfay ichwhay ehay allshay ebay osenchay. 
 
Ethay Ice-Presidentvay ofyay ethay Unitedyay Atesstay allshay ebay Esidentpray ofyay ethay Enatesay, 
utbay allshay avehay onay Otevay, unlessyay eythay ebay equallyyay ividedday. 
 
Ethay Enatesay allshay oosechay eirthay otheryay Officersyay, andyay alsoyay ayay Esidentpray 
opray emporetay, inyay ethay Absenceyay ofyay ethay Ice-Presidentvay, oryay enwhay ehay allshay 
exerciseyay ethay Officeyay ofyay Esidentpray ofyay ethay Unitedyay Atesstay. 
 
Ethay Enatesay allshay avehay ethay olesay Owerpay otay ytray allyay Impeachmentsyay. 
Enwhay ittingsay orfay atthay Urposepay, eythay allshay ebay onyay Oathyay oryay Affirmationyay. 
Enwhay ethay Esidentpray ofyay ethay Unitedyay Atesstay isyay iedtray, ethay Iefchay Usticejay 
allshay esidepray: Andyay onay Ersonpay allshay ebay onvictedcay ithoutway ethay Oncurrencecay 
ofyay otway irdsthay ofyay ethay Embersmay esentpray. 
 
Udgmentjay inyay asescay ofyay Impeachmentyay allshay otnay extendyay urtherfay anthay otay emovalray 
omfray Officeyay, andyay isqualificationday otay oldhay andyay enjoyyay anyyay Officeyay ofyay onorhay, 
Usttray oryay Ofitpray underyay ethay Unitedyay Atesstay:  utbay ethay Artypay onvictedcay allshay 
everthelessnay ebay iablelay andyay ubjectsay otay Indictmentyay, Ialtray, Udgmentjay andyay 
Unishmentpay, accordingyay otay Awlay. 
 
Ectionsay 4.  Ethay Imestay, Acesplay andyay Annermay ofyay oldinghay Electionsyay orfay Enatorssay andyay 
Epresentativesray, allshay ebay escribedpray inyay eachyay Atestay ybay ethay Egislaturelay ereofthay; 
utbay ethay Ongresscay aymay atyay anyyay imetay ybay Awlay akemay oryay alteryay uchsay Egulationsray, 
exceptyay asyay otay ethay Acesplay ofyay usingchay Enatorssay. 
 
Ethay Ongresscay allshay assembleyay atyay eastlay onceyay inyay everyyay Earyay, 
andyay uchsay Eetingmay allshay ebay onyay ethay irstfay Ondaymay inyay Ecemberday, 
unlessyay eythay allshay ybay awlay appointyay ayay ifferentday Ayday. 
 
 
Ectionsay 5.  Eachyay Ousehay allshay ebay ethay Udgejay ofyay ethay Electionsyay, 
Eturnsray andyay Ualificationsqay ofyay itsyay ownyay Embersmay, andyay ayay 
Ajoritymay ofyay eachyay allshay onstitutecay ayay Uorumqay otay oday Usinessbay; 
utbay ayay allersmay Umbernay aymay adjournyay omfray ayday otay ayday, 
andyay aymay ebay authorizedyay otay ompelcay ethay Attendanceyay ofyay absentyay Embersmay, 
inyay uchsay Annermay, andyay underyay uchsay Enaltiespay asyay eachyay Ousehay aymay ovidepray. 
 
Eachyay ousehay aymay etermineday ethay Ulesray ofyay itsyay Oceedingspray, 
unishpay itsyay Embersmay orfay isorderlyday Ehaviorbay, andyay, ithway ethay 
Oncurrencecay ofyay o-thirdstway, expelyay ayay Embermay. 
 
Eachyay ousehay allshay eepkay ayay Ournaljay ofyay itsyay Oceedingspray, 
andyay omfray imetay otay imetay ublishpay ethay amesay, exceptingyay uchsay Artspay asyay aymay 
inyay eirthay Udgmentjay equireray Ecrecysay; andyay ethay Easyay andyay Aysnay ofyay ethay 
Embersmay ofyay eitheryay Ousehay onyay anyyay uestionqay allshay, atyay ethay Esireday ofyay 
oneyay ifthfay ofyay osethay Esentpray, ebay enteredyay onyay ethay Ournaljay. 
 
Eithernay Ousehay, uringday ethay Essionsay ofyay Ongresscay, allshay, ithoutway ethay 
Onsentcay ofyay ethay otheryay, adjournyay orfay oremay anthay eethray aysday, ornay otay 
anyyay otheryay Aceplay anthay atthay inyay ichwhay ethay otway Ouseshay allshay ebay ittingsay. 
 
Ectionsay 6.  Ethay Enatorssay andyay Epresentativesray allshay eceiveray ayay Ompensationcay 
orfay eirthay Ervicessay, otay ebay ascertainedyay ybay Awlay, andyay aidpay outyay ofyay ethay Easurytray 
ofyay ethay Unitedyay Atesstay.  Eythay allshay inyay allyay Asescay, exceptyay Easontray, Elonyfay andyay 
Eachbray ofyay ethay Eacepay, ebay ivilegedpray omfray Arrestyay uringday eirthay Attendanceyay 
atyay ethay Essionsay ofyay eirthay espectiveray Ouseshay, andyay inyay oinggay otay andyay eturningray 
omfray ethay amesay; andyay orfay anyyay Eechspay oryay Ebateday inyay eitheryay Ousehay, 
eythay allshay otnay ebay uestionedqay inyay anyyay otheryay Aceplay. 
 
Onay Enatorsay oryay Epresentativeray allshay, uringday ethay Imetay orfay ichwhay ehay asway electedyay, 
ebay appointedyay otay anyyay ivilcay Officeyay underyay ethay authorityyay ofyay ethay Unitedyay Atesstay, 
ichwhay allshay avehay eenbay eatedcray, oryay ethay Emolumentsyay ereofwhay allshay avehay eenbay 
increasedyay uringday uchsay imetay; andyay onay Ersonpay oldinghay anyyay Officeyay underyay ethay 
Unitedyay Atesstay, allshay ebay ayay Embermay ofyay eitheryay Ousehay uringday ishay Ontinuancecay 
inyay Officeyay. 
 
Ectionsay 7.  Allyay Illsbay orfay aisingray Evenueray allshay originateyay inyay ethay 
Ousehay ofyay Epresentativesray; utbay ethay Enatesay aymay oposepray oryay oncurcay ithway 
Amendmentsyay asyay onyay otheryay Illsbay. 
 
Everyyay Illbay ichwhay allshay avehay assedpay ethay Ousehay ofyay Epresentativesray andyay 
ethay Enatesay, allshay, eforebay ityay ecomebay ayay Awlay, ebay esentedpray otay ethay 
Esidentpray ofyay ethay Unitedyay Atesstay; Ifyay ehay approveyay ehay allshay ignsay ityay, 
utbay ifyay otnay ehay allshay eturnray ityay, ithway ishay Objectionsyay otay atthay Ousehay 
inyay ichwhay ityay allshay avehay originatedyay, owhay allshay enteryay ethay Objectionsyay 
atyay argelay onyay eirthay Ournaljay, andyay oceedpray otay econsiderray ityay. 
Ifyay afteryay uchsay Econsiderationray otway irdsthay ofyay atthay ousehay 
allshay agreeyay otay asspay ethay Illbay, ityay allshay ebay entsay, 
ogethertay ithway ethay Objectionsyay, otay ethay otheryay Ousehay, ybay ichwhay 
ityay allshay ikewiselay ebay econsideredray, andyay ifyay approvedyay ybay otway irdsthay 
ofyay atthay Ousehay, ityay allshay ecomebay ayay awlay.  Utbay inyay allyay uchsay Asescay 
ethay Otesvay ofyay othbay Ouseshay allshay ebay eterminedday ybay Easyay andyay Aysnay, 
andyay ethay Amesnay ofyay ethay Ersonspay otingvay orfay andyay againstyay ethay Illbay allshay ebay 
enteredyay onyay ethay Ournaljay ofyay eachyay Ousehay espectivelyray.  Ifyay anyyay Illbay 
allshay otnay ebay eturnedray ybay ethay Esidentpray ithinway entay Aysday (Undayssay exceptedyay) 
afteryay ityay allshay avehay eenbay esentedpray otay imhay, ethay Amesay allshay ebay ayay Awlay, 
inyay ikelay Annermay asyay ifyay ehay adhay ignedsay ityay, unlessyay ethay Ongresscay ybay eirthay 
Adjournmentyay eventpray itsyay Eturnray, inyay ichwhay asecay ityay allshay otnay ebay ayay Awlay. 
 
Everyyay Orderyay, Esolutionray, oryay Otevay otay ichwhay ethay Oncurrencecay ofyay ethay Enatesay 
andyay Ousehay ofyay Epresentativesray aymay ebay ecessarynay (exceptyay onyay ayay uestionqay 
ofyay Adjournmentyay) allshay ebay esentedpray otay ethay Esidentpray ofyay ethay Unitedyay Atesstay; 
andyay eforebay ethay Amesay allshay aketay Effectyay, allshay ebay approvedyay ybay imhay, 
oryay eingbay isapprovedday ybay imhay, allshay ebay epassedray ybay otway irdsthay ofyay 
ethay Enatesay andyay Ousehay ofyay Epresentativesray, accordingyay otay ethay Ulesray 
andyay Imitationslay escribedpray inyay ethay Asecay ofyay ayay Illbay. 
 
Ectionsay 8.  Ethay Ongresscay allshay avehay Owerpay otay aylay andyay ollectcay Axestay, Utiesday, 
Impostsyay andyay Excisesyay, otay aypay ethay Ebtsday andyay ovidepray orfay ethay ommoncay Efenceday 
andyay eneralgay Elfareway ofyay ethay Unitedyay Atesstay; utbay allyay Utiesday, Impostsyay andyay Excisesyay 
allshay ebay uniformyay oughoutthray ethay Unitedyay Atesstay; 
 
Otay orrowbay Oneymay onyay ethay editcray ofyay ethay Unitedyay Atesstay; 
 
Otay egulateray Ommercecay ithway oreignfay Ationsnay, andyay amongyay ethay everalsay Atesstay, 
andyay ithway ethay Indianyay Ibestray; 
 
Otay establishyay anyay uniformyay Uleray ofyay Aturalizationnay, andyay uniformyay Awslay 
onyay ethay ubjectsay ofyay Ankruptciesbay oughoutthray ethay Unitedyay Atesstay; 
 
Otay oincay Oneymay, egulateray ethay Aluevay ereofthay, andyay ofyay oreignfay Oincay, 
andyay ixfay ethay Andardstay ofyay Eightsway andyay Easuresmay; 
 
Otay ovidepray orfay ethay Unishmentpay ofyay ounterfeitingcay ethay Ecuritiessay 
andyay urrentcay Oincay ofyay ethay Unitedyay Atesstay; 
 
Otay establishyay Ostpay Officesyay andyay Ostpay Oadsray; 
 
Otay omotepray ethay Ogresspray ofyay Iencescay andyay usefulyay Artsyay, ybay ecuringsay 
orfay imitedlay Imestay otay Authorsyay andyay Inventorsyay ethay exclusiveyay Ightray 
otay eirthay espectiveray Itingswray andyay Iscoveriesday; 
 
Otay onstitutecay Ibunalstray inferioryay otay ethay upremesay Ourtcay; 
 
Otay efineday andyay unishpay Iraciespay andyay Eloniesfay ommittedcay onyay ethay ighhay Eassay, 
andyay Offensesyay againstyay ethay Awlay ofyay Ationsnay; 
 
Otay eclareday Arway, antgray Etterslay ofyay Arquemay andyay Eprisalray, 
andyay akemay Ulesray oncerningcay Apturescay onyay Andlay andyay Aterway; 
 
Otay aiseray andyay upportsay Armiesyay, utbay onay Appropriationyay ofyay Oneymay otay atthay Useyay 
allshay ebay orfay ayay ongerlay ermtay anthay otway Earsyay; 
 
Otay ovidepray andyay aintainmay ayay Avynay; 
 
Otay akemay Ulesray orfay ethay Overnmentgay andyay Egulationray ofyay ethay andlay andyay avalnay Orcesfay; 
 
Otay ovidepray orfay allingcay orthfay ethay Ilitiamay otay executeyay ethay Awslay ofyay ethay Unionyay, 
uppresssay Insurrectionsyay andyay epelray Invasionsyay; 
 
Otay ovidepray orfay organizingyay, armingyay, andyay iscipliningday, ethay Ilitiamay, andyay orfay 
overninggay uchsay Artpay ofyay emthay asyay aymay ebay employedyay inyay ethay Ervicesay ofyay ethay 
Unitedyay Atesstay, eservingray otay ethay Atesstay espectivelyray, ethay Appointmentyay 
ofyay ethay Officersyay, andyay ethay Authorityyay ofyay ainingtray ethay ilitiamay accordingyay 
otay ethay isciplineday escribedpray ybay Ongresscay; 
 
Otay exerciseyay exclusiveyay Egislationlay inyay allyay Asescay atsoeverwhay, 
overyay uchsay Istrictday (otnay exceedingyay entay Ilesmay uaresqay) asyay aymay, 
ybay Essioncay ofyay articularpay Atesstay, andyay ethay Acceptanceyay ofyay Ongresscay, 
ecomebay ethay Eatsay ofyay ethay Overnmentgay ofyay ethay Unitedyay Atesstay, andyay otay 
exerciseyay ikelay Authorityyay overyay allyay Acesplay urchasedpay ybay ethay Onsentcay 
ofyay ethay Egislaturelay ofyay ethay Atestay inyay ichwhay ethay Amesay allshay ebay, 
orfay ethay Erectionyay ofyay Ortsfay, Agazinesmay, Arsenalsyay, Ockyardsday, 
andyay otheryay eedfulnay Uildings;--Andbay 
 
Otay akemay allyay Awslay ichwhay allshay ebay ecessarynay andyay operpray orfay arryingcay 
intoyay Executionyay ethay oregoingfay Owerspay, andyay allyay otheryay Owerspay estedvay 
ybay isthay Onstitutioncay inyay ethay Overnmentgay ofyay ethay Unitedyay Atesstay, 
oryay inyay anyyay Epartmentday oryay Officeryay ereofthay. 
 
Ectionsay 9.  Ethay Igrationmay oryay Importationyay ofyay uchsay Ersonspay asyay anyyay 
ofyay ethay Atesstay ownay existingyay allshay inkthay operpray otay admityay, allshay otnay 
ebay ohibitedpray ybay ethay Ongresscay iorpray otay ethay Earyay oneyay ousandthay eightyay 
undredhay andyay eightyay, utbay ayay Axtay oryay Utyday aymay ebay imposedyay onyay uchsay Importationyay, 
otnay exceedingyay entay ollarsday orfay eachyay Ersonpay. 
 
Ethay Ivilegepray ofyay ethay Itwray ofyay Abeashay Orpuscay allshay otnay ebay uspendedsay, unlessyay 
enwhay inyay Asescay ofyay Ebellionray oryay Invasionyay ethay ublicpay Afetysay aymay equireray ityay. 
 
Onay Illbay ofyay Attainderyay oryay exyay ostpay actofay Awlay allshay ebay assedpay. 
 
Onay Apitationcay, oryay otheryay irectday, Axtay allshay ebay aidlay, unlessyay inyay Oportionpray 
otay ethay Ensuscay oryay Enumerationyay ereinhay eforebay irectedday otay ebay akentay. 
 
Onay Axtay oryay Utyday allshay ebay aidlay onyay Articlesyay exportedyay omfray anyyay Atestay. 
 
Onay Eferencepray allshay ebay ivengay ybay anyyay Egulationray ofyay Ommercecay oryay Evenueray 
otay ethay Ortspay ofyay oneyay Atestay overyay osethay ofyay anotheryay: ornay allshay Esselsvay oundbay otay, 
oryay omfray, oneyay Atestay, ebay obligedyay otay enteryay, earclay, oryay aypay Utiesday inyay anotheryay. 
 
Onay Oneymay allshay ebay awndray omfray ethay Easurytray, utbay inyay Onsequencecay 
ofyay Appropriationsyay ademay ybay Awlay; andyay ayay egularray Atementstay andyay Accountyay 
ofyay ethay Eceiptsray andyay Expendituresyay ofyay allyay ublicpay Oneymay allshay ebay 
ublishedpay omfray imetay otay imetay. 
 
Onay Itletay ofyay Obilitynay allshay ebay antedgray ybay ethay Unitedyay Atesstay; 
andyay onay Ersonpay oldinghay anyyay Officeyay ofyay Ofitpray oryay Usttray underyay emthay, allshay, 
ithoutway ethay Onsentcay ofyay ethay Ongresscay, acceptyay ofyay anyyay esentpray, Emolumentyay, 
Officeyay, oryay Itletay, ofyay anyyay indkay ateverwhay, omfray anyyay Ingkay, Incepray, 
oryay oreignfay Atestay. 
 
Ectionsay 10.  Onay Atestay allshay enteryay intoyay anyyay Eatytray, Allianceyay, oryay 
Onfederationcay; antgray Etterslay ofyay Arquemay andyay Eprisalray; oincay Oneymay; 
emityay Illsbay ofyay Editcray; akemay anyyay Ingthay utbay oldgay andyay ilversay Oincay ayay Endertay 
inyay Aymentpay ofyay Ebtsday; asspay anyyay Illbay ofyay Attainderyay, exyay ostpay actofay Awlay, 
oryay Awlay impairingyay ethay Obligationyay ofyay Ontractscay, oryay antgray anyyay Itletay ofyay Obilitynay. 
 
Onay Atestay allshay, ithoutway ethay Onsentcay ofyay ethay Ongresscay, aylay anyyay Impostsyay oryay Utiesday 
onyay Importsyay oryay Exportsyay, exceptyay atwhay aymay ebay absolutelyyay ecessarynay orfay executingyay 
it'syay inspectionyay Awslay: andyay ethay etnay Oducepray ofyay allyay Utiesday andyay Impostsyay, 
aidlay ybay anyyay Atestay onyay Importsyay oryay Exportsyay, allshay ebay orfay ethay Useyay ofyay ethay Easurytray 
ofyay ethay Unitedyay Atesstay; andyay allyay uchsay Awslay allshay ebay ubjectsay otay ethay Evisionray 
andyay Ontroulcay ofyay ethay Ongresscay. 
 
 
Onay Atestay allshay, ithoutway ethay Onsentcay ofyay Ongresscay, aylay anyyay Utyday ofyay 
Onnagetay, eepkay Oopstray, oryay Ipsshay ofyay Arway inyay imetay ofyay Eacepay, enteryay intoyay anyyay 
Agreementyay oryay Ompactcay ithway anotheryay Atestay, oryay ithway ayay oreignfay Owerpay, oryay 
engageyay inyay Arway, unlessyay actuallyyay invadedyay, oryay inyay uchsay imminentyay Angerday 
asyay illway otnay admityay ofyay elayday. 
 
RTICLEAAY 2 
 
Ectionsay 1.  Ethay executiveyay Owerpay allshay ebay estedvay inyay ayay Esidentpray 
ofyay ethay Unitedyay Atesstay ofyay Americayay.  Ehay allshay oldhay ishay Officeyay uringday 
ethay Ermtay ofyay ourfay Earsyay, andyay, ogethertay ithway ethay Icevay Esidentpray 
osenchay orfay ethay amesay Ermtay, ebay electedyay, asyay ollowsfay: 
 
Eachyay Atestay allshay appointyay, inyay uchsay Annermay asyay ethay Egislaturelay ereofthay aymay irectday, 
ayay Umbernay ofyay Electorsyay, equalyay otay ethay olewhay Umbernay ofyay Enatorssay andyay Epresentativesray 
otay ichwhay ethay Atestay aymay ebay entitledyay inyay ethay Ongresscay:  utbay onay Enatorsay oryay 
Epresentativeray, oryay Ersonpay oldinghay anyay Officeyay ofyay Usttray oryay Ofitpray underyay 
ethay Unitedyay Atesstay, allshay ebay appointedyay anyay Electoryay. 
 
Ethay Electorsyay allshay eetmay inyay eirthay espectiveray Atesstay, andyay otevay ybay Allotbay 
orfay otway Ersonspay, ofyay omwhay oneyay atyay eastlay allshay otnay ielay anyay Inhabitantyay ofyay 
ethay amesay Atestay ithway emselvesthay.  Andyay eythay allshay akemay ayay Istlay ofyay 
allyay ethay Ersonspay otedvay orfay, andyay ofyay ethay Umbernay ofyay Otesvay orfay eachyay; 
ichwhay Istlay eythay allshay ignsay andyay ertifycay, andyay ansmittray ealedsay otay 
ethay Eatsay ofyay ethay Overnmentgay ofyay ethay Unitedyay Atesstay, irectedday otay ethay 
Esidentpray ofyay ethay Enatesay.  Ethay Esidentpray ofyay ethay Enatesay allshay, 
inyay ethay Esencepray ofyay ethay Enatesay andyay Ousehay ofyay Epresentativesray, 
openyay allyay ethay Ertificatescay, andyay ethay Otesvay allshay enthay ebay ountedcay. 
Ethay Ersonpay avinghay ethay eatestgray Umbernay ofyay Otesvay allshay ebay ethay Esidentpray, 
ifyay uchsay Umbernay ebay ayay Ajoritymay ofyay ethay olewhay Umbernay ofyay Electorsyay appointedyay; 
andyay ifyay erethay ebay oremay anthay oneyay owhay avehay uchsay Ajoritymay, andyay avehay anyay equalyay 
Umbernay ofyay otesvay, enthay ethay Ousehay ofyay Epresentativesray allshay immediatelyyay 
usechay ybay Allotbay oneyay ofyay emthay orfay Esidentpray; andyay ifyay onay Ersonpay avehay 
ayay Ajoritymay, enthay omfray ethay ivefay ighesthay onyay ethay Istlay ethay aidsay Ousehay 
allshay inyay ikelay Annermay usechay ethay Esidentpray.  Utbay inyay usingchay ethay Esidentpray, 
ethay Otesvay allshay ebay akentay ybay Atesstay, ethay Epresentationray omfray eachyay Atestay 
avinghay oneyay Otevay; ayay Uorumqay orfay isthay Urposepay allshay onsistcay ofyay ayay Embermay 
oryay Embersmay omfray otway irdsthay ofyay ethay Atesstay, andyay ayay Ajoritymay ofyay allyay ethay 
Atesstay allshay ebay ecessarynay otay ayay Oicechay.  Inyay everyyay Asecay, afteryay ethay Oicechay 
ofyay ethay Esidentpray, ethay Ersonpay avinghay ethay eatestgray Umbernay ofyay Otesvay ofyay 
ethay Electorsyay allshay ebay ethay Icevay Esidentpray.  Utbay ifyay erethay ouldshay emainray 
otway oryay oremay owhay avehay equalyay Otesvay, ethay Enatesay allshay usechay omfray emthay 
ybay Allotbay ethay Icevay Esidentpray. 
 
Ethay Ongresscay aymay etermineday ethay Imetay ofyay usingchay ethay Electorsyay, 
andyay ethay Ayday onyay ichwhay eythay allshay ivegay eirthay Otesvay; ichwhay Ayday 
allshay ebay ethay amesay oughoutthray ethay Unitedyay Atesstay. 
 
Onay Ersonpay exceptyay ayay aturalnay ornbay Itizencay, oryay ayay Itizencay ofyay ethay Unitedyay Atesstay, 
atyay ethay imetay ofyay ethay Adoptionyay ofyay isthay Onstitutioncay, allshay ebay eligibleyay otay 
ethay Officeyay ofyay Esidentpray; eithernay allshay anyyay Ersonpay ebay eligibleyay otay atthay 
Officeyay owhay allshay otnay avehay attainedyay otay ethay Ageyay ofyay irtythay ivefay Earsyay, 
andyay eenbay ourteenfay Earsyay ayay Esidentray ithinway ethay Unitedyay Atesstay. 
 
Inyay Asecay ofyay ethay Emovalray ofyay ethay Esidentpray omfray Officeyay, oryay ofyay ishay Eathday, 
Esignationray, oryay Inabilityyay otay ischargeday ethay Owerspay andyay Utiesday ofyay ethay 
aidsay Officeyay, ethay Amesay allshay evolveday onyay ethay Icevay Esidentpray, andyay ethay 
Ongresscay aymay ybay Awlay ovidepray orfay ethay Asecay ofyay Emovalray, Eathday, Esignationray 
oryay Inabilityyay, othbay ofyay ethay Esidentpray andyay Icevay Esidentpray, eclaringday atwhay 
Officeryay allshay enthay actyay asyay Esidentpray, andyay uchsay Officeryay allshay actyay accordinglyyay, 
untilyay ethay Isabilityday ebay emovedray, oryay ayay Esidentpray allshay ebay electedyay. 
 
Ethay Esidentpray allshay, atyay atedstay Imestay, eceiveray orfay ishay Ervicessay, 
ayay Ompensationcay, ichwhay allshay eithernay ebay encreasedyay ornay iminishedday uringday 
ethay Eriodpay orfay ichwhay ehay allshay avehay eenbay electedyay, andyay ehay allshay otnay eceiveray 
ithinway atthay Eriodpay anyyay otheryay Emolumentyay omfray ethay Unitedyay Atesstay, oryay anyyay ofyay emthay. 
 
Eforebay ehay enteryay onyay ethay Executionyay ofyay ishay Officeyay, ehay allshay aketay ethay 
ollowingfay Oathyay oryay Affirmation:--"Iyay oday olemnlysay earsway (oryay affirmyay) atthay 
Iyay illway aithfullyfay executeyay ethay Officeyay ofyay Esidentpray ofyay ethay Unitedyay Atesstay, 
andyay illway otay ethay estbay ofyay ymay Abilityyay, eservepray, otectpray andyay efendday ethay 
Onstitutioncay ofyay ethay Unitedyay Atesstay." 
 
Ectionsay 2.  Ethay Esidentpray allshay ebay Ommandercay inyay Iefchay ofyay ethay Armyyay 
andyay Avynay ofyay ethay Unitedyay Atesstay, andyay ofyay ethay Ilitiamay ofyay ethay everalsay Atesstay, 
enwhay alledcay intoyay ethay actualyay Ervicesay ofyay ethay Unitedyay Atesstay; 
ehay aymay equireray ethay Opinionyay, inyay itingwray, ofyay ethay incipalpray Officeryay 
inyay eachyay ofyay ethay executiveyay Epartmentsday, uponyay anyyay Ubjectsay elatingray otay 
ethay Utiesday ofyay eirthay espectiveray Officesyay, andyay ehay allshay avehay Owerpay 
otay antgray Eprievesray andyay Ardonspay orfay Offensesyay againstyay ethay Unitedyay Atesstay, 
exceptyay inyay Asescay ofyay impeachmentyay. 
 
Ehay allshay avehay Owerpay, ybay andyay ithway ethay Adviceyay andyay Onsentcay ofyay ethay 
Enatesay, otay akemay Eatiestray, ovidedpray otway irdsthay ofyay ethay Enatorssay 
esentpray oncurcay; andyay ehay allshay ominatenay, andyay ybay andyay ithway ethay Adviceyay 
andyay Onsentcay ofyay ethay Enatesay, allshay appointyay Ambassadorsyay, otheryay ublicpay 
Inistersmay andyay Onsulscay, Udgesjay ofyay ethay upremesay Ourtcay, andyay allyay otheryay 
Officersyay ofyay ethay Unitedyay Atesstay, osewhay Appointmentsyay areyay otnay ereinhay 
otherwiseyay ovidedpray orfay, andyay ichwhay allshay ebay establishedyay ybay Awlay: 
utbay ethay Ongresscay aymay ybay Awlay estvay ethay Appointmentyay ofyay uchsay inferioryay Officersyay, 
asyay eythay inkthay operpray, inyay ethay Esidentpray aloneyay, inyay ethay Ourtscay ofyay Awlay, 
oryay inyay ethay Eadshay ofyay Epartmentsday. 
 
Ethay Esidentpray allshay avehay Owerpay otay illfay upyay allyay Acanciesvay atthay aymay appenhay 
uringday ethay Ecessray ofyay ethay Enatesay, ybay antinggray Ommissionscay ichwhay allshay 
expireyay atyay ethay Endyay ofyay eirthay extnay essionsay. 
 
Ectionsay 3.  Ehay allshay omfray imetay otay imetay ivegay otay ethay Ongresscay 
Informationyay ofyay ethay Atestay ofyay ethay Unionyay, andyay ecommendray otay eirthay 
Onsiderationcay uchsay Easuresmay asyay ehay allshay udgejay ecessarynay andyay expedientyay; 
ehay aymay, onyay extraordinaryyay Occasionsyay, onvenecay othbay Ouseshay, oryay eitheryay 
ofyay emthay, andyay inyay Asecay ofyay Isagreementday etweenbay emthay, ithway Espectray otay 
ethay Imetay ofyay Adjournmentyay, ehay aymay adjournyay emthay otay uchsay Imetay asyay ehay allshay 
inkthay operpray; ehay allshay eceiveray Ambassadorsyay andyay otheryay ublicpay Inistersmay; 
ehay allshay aketay Arecay atthay ethay Awslay ebay aithfullyfay executedyay, andyay allshay 
Ommissioncay allyay ethay Officersyay ofyay ethay Unitedyay Atesstay. 
 
Ectionsay 4.  Ethay Esidentpray, Icevay Esidentpray andyay allyay ivilcay Officersyay ofyay ethay 
Unitedyay Atesstay, allshay ebay emovedray omfray Officeyay onyay Impeachmentyay orfay, 
andyay Onvictioncay ofyay, Easontray, Iberybray, oryay otheryay ighhay Imescray andyay Isdemeanorsmay. 
 
RTICLEAAY HREETAY 
 
Ectionsay 1.  Ethay udicialjay Owerpay ofyay ethay Unitedyay Atesstay, allshay ebay estedvay 
inyay oneyay upremesay Ourtcay, andyay inyay uchsay inferioryay Ourtscay asyay ethay Ongresscay aymay 
omfray imetay otay imetay ordainyay andyay establishyay.  Ethay Udgesjay, othbay ofyay ethay upremesay 
andyay inferioryay Ourtscay, allshay oldhay eirthay Officesyay uringday oodgay ehaviorbay, 
andyay allshay, atyay atedstay Imestay, eceiveray orfay eirthay Ervicessay, ayay Ompensationcay, 
ichwhay allshay otnay ebay iminishedday uringday eirthay Ontinuancecay inyay Officeyay. 
 
Ectionsay 2.  Ethay udicialjay Owerpay allshay extendyay otay allyay Asescay, inyay Awlay andyay Equityyay, 
arisingyay underyay isthay Onstitutioncay, ethay Awslay ofyay ethay Unitedyay Atesstay, andyay Eatiestray 
ademay, oryay ichwhay allshay ebay ademay, underyay eirthay Authority;--toyay allyay Asescay affectingyay 
Ambassadorsyay, otheryay ublicpay Inistersmay andyay Onsuls;--tocay allyay Asescay ofyay admiraltyyay 
andyay aritimemay Urisdiction;--tojay Ontroversiescay otay ichwhay ethay Unitedyay Atesstay 
allshay ebay ayay Arty;--topay Ontroversiescay etweenbay otway oryay oremay Ates;--betweenstay ayay 
Atestay andyay Itizenscay ofyay anotheryay Ate;--betweenstay Itizenscay ofyay ifferentday Atesstay; 
etween--bay Itizenscay ofyay ethay amesay Atestay aimingclay Andslay underyay Antsgray ofyay 
ifferentday Atesstay, andyay etweenbay ayay Atestay, oryay ethay Itizenscay ereofthay, 
andyay oreignfay Atesstay, Itizenscay oryay Ubjectssay. 
 
Inyay allyay asescay affectingyay Ambassadorsyay, otheryay ublicpay Inistersmay andyay Onsulscay, 
andyay osethay inyay ichwhay ayay Atestay allshay ebay Artypay, ethay upremesay Ourtcay allshay avehay 
originalyay Urisdictionjay.  Inyay allyay ethay otheryay Asescay eforebay entionedmay, ethay 
upremesay Ourtcay allshay avehay appellateyay Urisdictionjay, othbay asyay otay Awlay andyay Actfay, 
ithway uchsay Exceptionsyay, andyay underyay uchsay Egulationsray asyay ethay Ongresscay allshay akemay. 
 
Ethay Ialtray ofyay allyay Imescray, exceptyay inyay Asescay ofyay Impeachmentyay, allshay ebay ybay Uryjay; 
andyay uchsay Ialtray allshay ebay eldhay inyay ethay Atestay erewhay ethay aidsay Imescray allshay 
avehay eenbay ommittedcay; utbay enwhay otnay ommittedcay ithinway anyyay Atestay, ethay Ialtray 
allshay ebay atyay uchsay Aceplay oryay Acesplay asyay ethay Ongresscay aymay ybay Awlay avehay irectedday. 
 
Ectionsay 3.  Easontray againstyay ethay Unitedyay Atesstay, allshay onsistcay onlyyay inyay 
evyinglay Arway againstyay emthay, oryay inyay adheringyay otay eirthay Enemiesyay, ivinggay emthay 
Aidyay andyay Omfortcay.  Onay Ersonpay allshay ebay onvictedcay ofyay Easontray unlessyay onyay 
ethay Estimonytay ofyay otway Itnessesway otay ethay amesay overtyay Actyay, oryay onyay Onfessioncay 
inyay openyay Ourtcay. 
 
Ethay Ongresscay allshay avehay owerpay otay eclareday ethay unishmentpay ofyay Easontray, 
utbay onay Attainderyay ofyay Easontray allshay orkway Orruptioncay ofyay Oodblay, 
oryay Orfeiturefay exceptyay uringday ethay Ifelay ofyay ethay Ersonpay attaintedyay. 
 
 
RTICLEAAY OURFAY 
 
Ectionsay 1.  Ullfay Aithfay andyay Editcray allshay ebay ivengay inyay eachyay Atestay otay ethay 
ublicpay Actsyay, Ecordsray, andyay udicialjay Oceedingspray ofyay everyyay otheryay Atestay. 
Andyay ethay Ongresscay aymay ybay eneralgay Awslay escribepray ethay Annermay inyay ichwhay uchsay Actsyay, 
Ecordsray, andyay Oceedingspray allshay ebay ovedpray, andyay ethay Effectyay ereofthay. 
 
 
Ectionsay 2.  Ethay Itizenscay ofyay eachyay Atestay allshay ebay entitledyay otay allyay 
Ivilegespray andyay Immunitiesyay ofyay Itizenscay inyay ethay everalsay Atesstay. 
 
Ayay Ersonpay argedchay inyay anyyay Atestay ithway Easontray, Elonyfay, oryay otheryay Imecray, 
owhay allshay eeflay omfray Usticejay, andyay ebay oundfay inyay anotheryay Atestay, 
allshay onyay Emandday ofyay ethay executiveyay Authorityyay ofyay ethay Atestay omfray 
ichwhay ehay edflay, ebay eliveredday upyay, otay ebay emovedray otay ethay Atestay avinghay 
Urisdictionjay ofyay ethay Imecray. 
 
Onay ersonpay eldhay otay Ervicesay oryay Aborlay inyay oneyay Atestay, underyay ethay Awslay ereofthay, 
escapingyay intoyay anotheryay, allshay, inyay Onsequencecay ofyay anyyay Awlay oryay Egulationray ereinthay, 
ebay ischargedday omfray uchsay Ervicesay oryay Aborlay, Utbay allshay ebay eliveredday upyay onyay Aimclay 
ofyay ethay Artypay otay omwhay uchsay Ervicesay oryay Aborlay aymay ebay ueday. 
 
 
Ectionsay 3.  Ewnay Atesstay aymay ebay admittedyay ybay ethay Ongresscay intoyay isthay Unionyay; 
utbay onay ewnay Atesstay allshay ebay ormedfay oryay erectedyay ithinway ethay Urisdictionjay 
ofyay anyyay otheryay Atestay; ornay anyyay Atestay ebay ormedfay ybay ethay Unctionjay ofyay otway 
oryay oremay Atesstay, oryay Artspay ofyay Atesstay, ithoutway ethay Onsentcay ofyay ethay 
Egislatureslay ofyay ethay Atesstay oncernedcay asyay ellway asyay ofyay ethay Ongresscay. 
 
Ethay Ongresscay allshay avehay Owerpay otay isposeday ofyay andyay akemay allyay eedfulnay Ulesray 
andyay Egulationsray espectingray ethay Erritorytay oryay otheryay Opertypray elongingbay 
otay ethay Unitedyay Atesstay; andyay othingnay inyay isthay Onstitutioncay allshay ebay osay 
onstruedcay asyay otay Ejudicepray anyyay Aimsclay ofyay ethay Unitedyay Atesstay, 
oryay ofyay anyyay articularpay Atestay. 
 
Ectionsay 4.  Ethay Unitedyay Atesstay allshay uaranteegay otay everyyay Atestay inyay isthay Unionyay 
ayay Epublicanray Ormfay ofyay Overnmentgay, andyay allshay otectpray eachyay ofyay emthay againstyay 
Invasionyay; andyay onyay Applicationyay ofyay ethay Egislaturelay, oryay ofyay ethay Executiveyay 
(enwhay ethay Egislaturelay annotcay ebay onvenedcay) againstyay omesticday Iolencevay.

allshay oposepray Amendmentsyay otay isthay Onstitutioncay, oryay, onyay ethay Applicationyay ofyay

Ivegay Emay Ibertylay Oryay Ivegay Emay Eathday 
 
 
Atrickpay Enryhay, Archmay 23, 1775. 
 
 
Onay anmay inksthay oremay ighlyhay anthay Iyay oday ofyay ethay atriotismpay, asyay ellway asyay abilitiesyay, 
ofyay ethay eryvay orthyway entlemengay owhay avehay ustjay addressedyay ethay Ousehay.  Utbay ifferentday 
enmay oftenyay eesay ethay amesay ubjectsay inyay ifferentday ightslay; andyay, ereforethay, Iyay opehay ityay 
illway otnay ebay oughtthay isrespectfulday otay osethay entlemengay ifyay, entertainingyay asyay Iyay oday 
opinionsyay ofyay ayay aracterchay eryvay oppositeyay otay eirsthay, Iyay allshay eakspay orthfay ymay 
entimentssay eelyfray andyay ithoutway eserveray.  Isthay isyay onay imetay orfay eremonycay. 
Ethay uestingqay eforebay ethay Ousehay isyay oneyay ofyay awfulyay omentmay otay isthay ountrycay. 
Orfay ymay ownyay artpay, Iyay onsidercay ityay asyay othingnay esslay anthay ayay uestionqay ofyay 
eedomfray oryay averyslay; andyay inyay oportionpray otay ethay agnitudemay ofyay ethay ubjectsay 
oughtyay otay ebay ethay eedomfray ofyay ethay ebateday.  Ityay isyay onlyyay inyay isthay ayway atthay 
eway ancay opehay otay arriveyay atyay uthtray, andyay ulfillfay ethay eatgray esponsibilityray 
ichwhay eway oldhay otay Odgay andyay ouryay ountrycay.  Ouldshay Iyay eepkay ackbay ymay opinionsyay 
atyay uchsay ayay imetay, oughthray earfay ofyay ivinggay offenseyay, Iyay ouldshay onsidercay yselfmay 
asyay uiltygay ofyay easontray owardstay ymay ountrycay, andyay ofyay anyay actyay ofyay isloyaltyday 
owardtay ethay Ajestymay ofyay Eavenhay, ichwhay Iyay evereray aboveyay allyay earthlyyay ingskay. 
 
Rmay. Esidentpray, ityay isyay aturalnay otay anmay otay indulgeyay inyay ethay illusionsyay ofyay opehay. 
Eway areyay aptyay otay utshay ouryay eyesyay againstyay ayay ainfulpay uthtray, andyay istenlay otay ethay 
ongsay ofyay atthay irensay illtay eshay ansformstray usyay intoyay eastsbay.  Isyay isthay ethay artpay 
ofyay iseway enmay, engagedyay inyay ayay eatgray andyay arduousyay ugglestray orfay ibertylay? 
Areyay eway isposedday otay ebay ofyay ethay umbernay ofyay osethay owhay, avinghay eyesyay, eesay otnay, 
andyay, avinghay earsyay, earhay otnay, ethay ingsthay ichwhay osay earlynay oncerncay eirthay 
emporaltay alvationsay?  Orfay ymay artpay, ateverwhay anguishyay ofyay iritspay ityay aymay ostcay, 
Iyay amyay illingway otay owknay ethay olewhay uthtray; otay owknay ethay orstway, andyay otay ovidepray orfay ityay. 
 
Iyay avehay utbay oneyay amplay ybay ichwhay ymay eetfay areyay uidedgay, andyay atthay isyay ethay amplay ofyay 
experienceyay.  Iyay owknay ofyay onay ayway ofyay udgingjay ofyay ethay uturefay utbay ybay ethay astpay. 
Andyay udgingjay ybay ethay astpay, Iyay ishway otay owknay atwhay erethay ashay eenbay inyay ethay onductcay 
ofyay ethay Itishbray inistrymay orfay ethay astlay entay earsyay otay ustifyjay osethay opeshay ithway 
ichwhay  entlemengay avehay eenbay easedplay otay olacesay emselvesthay andyay ethay Ousehay. 
Isyay ityay atthay insidiousyay ilesmay ithway ichwhay ouryay etitionpay ashay eenbay atelylay eceivedray? 
Usttray ityay otnay, irsay; ityay illway ovepray ayay aresnay otay ouryay eetfay.  Uffersay otnay ourselvesyay 
otay ebay etrayedbay ithway ayay isskay.  Askyay ourselvesyay owhay isthay aciousgray eceptionray ofyay ouryay 
etitionpay omportscay ithway osethay arlikeway eparationspray ichwhay overcay ouryay atersway andyay 
arkenday ouryay andlay.  Areyay eetsflay andyay armiesyay ecessarynay otay ayay orkway ofyay ovelay andyay 
econciliationray?  Avehay eway ownshay ourselvesyay osay unwillingyay otay ebay econciledray atthay 
orcefay ustmay ebay alledcay inyay otay inway ackbay ouryay ovelay?  Etlay usyay otnay eceiveday ourselvesyay, 
irsay.  Esethay areyay ethay implementsyay ofyay arway andyay ubjugationsay; ethay astlay argumentsyay otay 
ichwhay ingskay esortray.  Iyay askyay entlemengay, irsay, atwhay eansmay isthay artialmay arrayyay, ifyay 
itsyay urposepay ebay otnay otay orcefay usyay otay ubmissionsay?  Ancay entlemengay assignyay anyyay otheryay 
ossiblepay otivemay orfay ityay?  Ashay Eatgray Itainbray anyyay enemyyay, inyay isthay uarterqay ofyay 
ethay orldway, otay allcay orfay allyay isthay accumulationyay ofyay aviesnay andyay armiesyay?  Onay, irsay, 
eshay ashay onenay.  Eythay areyay eantmay orfay usyay:  eythay ancay ebay eantmay orfay onay otheryay. 
Eythay areyay entsay overyay otay indbay andyay ivetray uponyay usyay osethay ainschay ichwhay ethay Itishbray 
inistrymay avehay eenbay osay onglay orgingfay.  Andyay atwhay avehay eway otay opposeyay otay emthay? 
Allshay eway ytray argumentyay?  Irsay, eway avehay eenbay yingtray atthay orfay ethay astlay entay earsyay. 
Avehay eway anythingyay ewnay otay offeryay uponyay ethay ubjectsay?  Othingnay.  Eway avehay eldhay ethay 
ubjectsay upyay inyay everyyay ightlay ofyay ichwhay ityay isyay apablecay; utbay ityay ashay eenbay allyay inyay ainvay. 
Allshay eway esortray otay entreatyyay andyay umblehay upplicationsay?  Atwhay ermstay allshay eway 
indfay ichwhay avehay otnay eenbay alreadyyay exhaustedyay?  Etlay usyay otnay, Iyay eseechbay ouyay, irsay, 
eceiveday ourselvesyay.  Irsay, eway avehay oneday everythingyay atthay ouldcay ebay oneday otay avertyay 
ethay ormstay ichwhay isyay ownay omingcay onyay.  Eway avehay etitionedpay; eway avehay emonstratedray; 
eway avehay upplicatedsay; eway avehay ostratedpray ourselvesyay eforebay ethay onethray, andyay avehay 
imploredyay itsyay interpositionyay otay arrestyay ethay yrannicaltay andshay ofyay ethay inistrymay andyay 
Arliamentpay.  Ouryay etitionspay avehay eenbay ightedslay; ouryay emonstrancesray avehay oducedpray 
additionalyay iolencevay andyay insultyay; ouryay upplicationssay avehay eenbay isregardedday; 
andyay eway avehay eenbay urnedspay, ithway ontemptcay, omfray ethay ootfay ofyay ethay onethray! 
Inyay ainvay, afteryay esethay ingsthay, aymay eway indulgeyay ethay ondfay opehay ofyay eacepay andyay 
econciliationray.  Erethay isyay onay ongerlay anyyay oomray orfay opehay.  Ifyay eway ishway otay ebay ee--fray 
ifyay eway eanmay otay eservepray inviolateyay osethay inestimableyay ivilegespray orfay ichwhay 
eway avehay eenbay osay onglay ontending--ifcay eway eanmay otnay aselybay otay abandonyay ethay oblenay 
ugglestray inyay ichwhay eway avehay eenbay osay onglay engagedyay, andyay ichwhay eway avehay edgedplay 
ourselvesyay evernay otay abandonyay untilyay ethay oriousglay objectyay ofyay ouryay ontestcay 
allshay ebay obtained--weyay ustmay ightfay!  Iyay epeatray ityay, irsay, eway ustmay ightfay! 
Anyay appealyay otay armsyay  andyay otay ethay Odgay ofyay ostshay isyay allyay atthay isyay eftlay usyay! 
 
Eythay elltay usyay, irsay, atthay eway areyay eakway; unableyay otay opecay ithway osay ormidablefay 
anyay adversaryyay.  Utbay enwhay allshay eway ebay ongerstray?  Illway ityay ebay ethay extnay eekway, 
oryay ethay extnay earyay?  Illway ityay ebay enwhay eway areyay otallytay isarmedday, andyay enwhay ayay Itishbray 
uardgay allshay ebay ationedstay inyay everyyay ousehay?  Allshay eway athergay engthstray utbay 
irresolutionyay andyay inactionyay?  Allshay eway acquireyay ethay eansmay ofyay effectualyay esistanceray 
ybay yinglay upinelysay onyay ouryay acksbay andyay ugginghay ethay elusiveday antomphay ofyay opehay, untilyay 
ouryay enemiesyay allshay avehay oundbay usyay andhay andyay ootfay?  Irsay, eway areyay otnay eakway ifyay eway akemay 
ayay operpray useyay ofyay osethay eansmay ichwhay ethay Odgay ofyay aturenay athhay acedplay inyay ouryay owerpay. 
Ethay illionsmay ofyay eoplepay, armedyay inyay ethay olyhay ausecay ofyay ibertylay, andyay inyay uchsay ayay 
ountrycay asyay atthay ichwhay eway ossesspay, areyay invincibleyay ybay anyyay orcefay ichwhay ouryay enemyyay 
ancay endsay againstyay usyay.  Esidesbay, irsay, eway allshay otnay ightfay ouryay attlesbay aloneyay. 
Erethay isyay ayay ustjay Odgay owhay esidespray overyay ethay estiniesday ofyay ationsnay, andyay owhay illway 
aiseray upyay iendsfray otay ightfay ouryay attlesbay orfay usyay.  Ethay attlebay, irsay, isyay otnay otay ethay 
ongstray aloneyay; ityay isyay otay ethay igilantvay, ethay activeyay, ethay avebray.  Esidesbay, irsay, 
eway avehay onay electionyay.  Ifyay eway ereway asebay enoughyay otay esireday ityay, ityay isyay ownay ootay atelay 
otay etireray omfray ethay ontestcay.  Erethay isyay onay etreatray utbay inyay ubmissionsay andyay averyslay! 
Ouryay ainschay areyay orgedfay!  Eirthay ankingclay aymay ebay eardhay onyay ethay ainsplay ofyay Ostonbay! 
Ethay arway isyay inevitable--andyay etlay ityay omecay!  Iyay epeatray ityay, irsay, etlay ityay omecay. 
 
Ityay isyay inyay ainvay, irsay, otay extenuateyay ethay attermay.  Entlemengay aymay ycray, Eacepay, Eace--pay 
utbay erethay isyay onay eacepay.  Ethay arway isyay actuallyyay egunbay!  Ethay extnay alegay atthay eepssway 
omfray ethay orthnay illway ingbray otay ouryay earsyay ethay ashclay ofyay esoundingray armsyay! 
Ouryay ethrenbray areyay alreadyyay inyay ethay ieldfay!  Ywhay andstay eway erehay idleyay? 
Atwhay isyay ityay atthay entlemengay ishway?  Atwhay ouldway eythay avehay?  Isyay ifelay osay earday, 
oryay eacepay osay eetsway, asyay otay ebay urchasedpay atyay ethay icepray ofyay ainschay andyay averyslay? 
Orbidfay ityay, Almightyyay Odgay!  Iyay owknay otnay atwhay oursecay othersyay aymay aketay; 
utbay asyay orfay emay, ivegay emay ibertylay oryay ivegay emay eathday!

Incoln'slay Econdsay Inauguralyay Addressyay 
Archmay 4, 1865 
 
 
 
Ellowfay ountrymencay:  Atyay isthay econdsay appearingyay otay aketay ethay oathyay 
ofyay ethay esidentialpray officeyay, erethay isyay esslay occasionyay orfay anyay extendedyay 
addressyay anthay erethay asway atyay ethay irstfay.  Enthay ayay atementstay, omewhatsay 
inyay etailday, ofyay ayay oursecay otay ebay ursuedpay, eemedsay ittingfay andyay operpray. 
Ownay, atyay ethay expirationyay ofyay ourfay earsyay, uringday ichwhay ublicpay eclarationsday 
avehay eenbay onstantlycay alledcay orthfay onyay everyyay ointpay andyay asephay ofyay ethay eatgray 
ontestcay ichwhay illstay absorbsyay ethay attentionyay andyay engrossesyay ethay energiesyay 
ofyay ethay ationnay, ittlelay atthay isyay ewnay ouldcay ebay esentedpray.  Ethay ogresspray 
ofyay ouryay armsyay, uponyay ichwhay allyay elseyay ieflychay ependsday, isyay asyay ellway ownknay 
otay ethay ublicpay asyay otay yselfmay; andyay ityay isyay, Iyay usttray, easonablyray atisfactorysay 
andyay encouragingyay otay allyay.  Ithway ighhay opehay orfay ethay uturefay, onay edictionpray 
inyay egardray otay ityay isyay enturedvay. 
 
Onyay ethay occasionyay orrespondingcay otay isthay ourfay earsyay agoyay, allyay oughtsthay 
ereway anxiouslyyay irectedday otay anyay impendingyay ivilcay arway.  Allyay eadeddray it--yay 
allyay oughtsay otay avertyay ityay.  Ilewhay ethay inauguralyay addressyay asway eingbay eliveredday 
omfray isthay aceplay, evotedday altogetheryay otay avingsay ethay Unionyay ithoutway arway, 
insurgentyay agentsyay ereway inyay ethay itycay eekingsay otay estroyday ityay ithoutway ar--way 
eekingsay otay issolveday ethay Unionyay, andyay ivideday effectsyay, ybay egotiationnay. 
Othbay artiespay eprecatedday arway; utbay oneyay ofyay emthay ouldway akemay arway atherray 
anthay etlay ethay ationnay urvivesay; andyay ethay otheryay ouldway acceptyay arway atherray 
anthay etlay ityay erishpay.  Andyay ethay arway amecay. 
 
One-eighthyay ofyay ethay olewhay opulationpay ereway oloredcay avesslay, otnay istributedday 
enerallygay overyay ethay Unionyay, utbay ocalizedlay inyay ethay Outhernsay artpay ofyay ityay. 
Esethay avesslay onstitutedcay ayay eculiarpay andyay owerfulpay interestyay.  Allyay ewknay 
atthay isthay interestyay asway, omehowsay, ethay ausecay ofyay ethay arway.  Otay engthenstray, 
erpetuatepay, andyay extendyay isthay interestyay asway ethay objectyay orfay ichwhay ethay 
insurgentsyay ouldway endray ethay Unionyay, evenyay ybay arway; ilewhay ethay overnmentgay aimedclay 
onay ightray otay oday oremay anthay otay estrictray ethay erritorialtay enlargementyay ofyay ityay. 
 
Eithernay artypay expectedyay orfay ethay arway ethay agnitudemay oryay ethay urationday 
ichwhay ityay ashay alreadyyay attainedyay.  Eithernay anticipatedyay atthay ethay ausecay 
ofyay ethay onflictcay ightmay easecay ithway, oryay evenyay eforebay, ethay onflictcay itselfyay 
ouldshay easecay.  Eachyay ookedlay orfay anyay easieryay iumphtray, andyay ayay esultray esslay 
undamentalfay andyay astoundingyay.  Othbay eadray ethay amesay Iblebay, andyay aypray 
otay ethay amesay Odgay; andyay eachyay invokesyay ishay aidyay againstyay ethay otheryay. 
Ityay aymay eemsay angestray atthay anyyay enmay ouldshay areday otay askyay ayay ustjay Od'sgay 
assistanceyay inyay ingingwray eirthay eadbray omfray ethay eatsway ofyay otheryay en'smay acesfay; 
utbay etlay usyay udgejay otnay, atthay eway ebay otnay udgedjay.  Ethay ayerspray ofyay othbay 
ouldcay otnay ebay answered--thatyay ofyay eithernay ashay eenbay answeredyay ullyfay. 
 
Ethay Almightyyay ashay ishay ownyay urposespay.  "Oeway untoyay ethay orldway ecausebay 
ofyay offensesyay! orfay ityay ustmay eedsnay ebay atthay offensesyay omecay; utbay oeway 
otay atthay anmay ybay omwhay ethay offenseyay omethcay."  Ifyay eway allshay upposesay 
atthay Americanyay averyslay isyay oneyay ofyay osethay offensesyay ichwhay, inyay ethay 
ovidencepray ofyay Odgay, ustmay eedsnay omecay, utbay ichwhay, avinghay ontinuedcay 
oughthray ishay appointedyay imetay, ehay ownay illsway otay emoveray, andyay atthay ehay 
ivesgay otay othbay Orthnay andyay Outhsay isthay erribletay arway, asyay ethay oeway ueday 
otay osethay ybay omwhay ethay offenseyay amecay, allshay eway iscernday ereinthay anyyay 
epartureday omfray osethay ivineday attributesyay ichwhay ethay elieversbay inyay ayay 
ivinglay Odgay alwaysyay ascribeyay otay imhay?  Ondlyfay oday eway ope--ferventlyhay 
oday eway ay--thatpray isthay ightymay ourgescay ofyay arway aymay eedilyspay asspay awayyay. 
Etyay, ifyay Odgay illsway atthay ityay ontinuecay untilyay allyay ethay ealthway iledpay ybay 
ethay ondsman'sbay otway undredhay andyay iftyfay earsyay ofyay unrequitedyay oiltay 
allshay ebay unksay, andyay untilyay everyyay opdray ofyay oodblay awndray ybay ethay ashlay 
allshay ebay aidpay ybay anotheryay awndray ithway ethay ordsway, asyay asway aidsay 
eethray ousandthay earsyay agoyay, osay illstay ityay ustmay ebay aidsay, "Ethay 
udgmentsjay ofyay ethay Ordlay areyay uetray andyay ighteousray altogetheryay." 
 
Ithway alicemay owardtay onenay; ithway aritychay orfay allyay; ithway irmnessfay inyay 
ethay ightray, asyay Odgay ivesgay usyay otay eesay ethay ightray, etlay usyay ivestray onyay 
otay inishfay ethay orkway eway areyay inyay; otay indbay upyay ethay ation'snay oundsway; 
otay arecay orfay imhay owhay allshay avehay ornebay ethay attlebay, andyay orfay ishay idowway, 
andyay ishay orphan--toyay oday allyay ichwhay aymay achieveyay andyay erishchay ayay ustjay 
andyay astinglay eacepay amongyay ourselvesyay, andyay ithway allyay ationsnay.

Incoln'slay Irstfay Inauguralyay Addressyay 
Archmay 4, 1861 
 
 
 
Ellowfay itizenscay ofyay ethay Unitedyay Atesstay:  inyay ompliancecay ithway ayay ustomcay asyay oldyay 
asyay ethay overnmentgay itselfyay, Iyay appearyay eforebay ouyay otay addressyay ouyay ieflybray 
andyay otay aketay, inyay ouryay esencepray, ethay oathyay escribedpray ybay ethay Onstitutioncay 
ofyay ethay Unitedyay Atesstay, otay ebay akentay ybay ethay Esidentpray "eforebay ehay entersyay 
onyay ethay executionyay ofyay ishay officeyay." 
 
Iyay oday otnay onsidercay ityay ecessarynay, atyay esentpray, orfay emay otay iscussday osethay attersmay 
ofyay administrationyay aboutyay ichwhay erethay isyay onay ecialspay anxietyyay, oryay excitementyay. 
 
Apprehensionyay eemssay otay existyay amongyay ethay eoplepay ofyay ethay Outhernsay Atesstay 
atthay ybay ethay accessionyay ofyay ayay Epublicanray administrationyay eirthay opertypray 
andyay eirthay eacepay andyay ersonalpay ecuritysay areyay otay ebay endangeredyay. 
Erethay ashay evernay eenbay anyyay easonableray ausecay orfay uchsay apprehensionyay. 
Indeedyay, ethay ostmay ampleyay evidenceyay otay ethay ontrarycay ashay allyay ethay ilewhay 
existedyay andyay eenbay openyay otay eirthay inspectionyay.  Ityay isyay oundfay inyay 
earlynay allyay ethay ublishedpay eechesspay ofyay imhay owhay ownay addressesyay ouyay. 
Iyay oday utbay uoteqay omfray oneyay ofyay osethay eechesspay enwhay Iyay eclareday atthay 
"Iyay avehay onay urposepay, irectlyday oryay indirectlyyay, otay interfereyay ithway 
ethay institutionyay ofyay averyslay erewhay ityay existsyay.  Iyay elievebay Iyay avehay 
onay awfullay ightray otay oday osay, andyay Iyay avehay onay inclinationyay otay oday osay." 
Osethay owhay ominatednay andyay electedyay emay idday osay ithway ullfay owledgeknay 
atthay Iyay adhay ademay isthay andyay anymay imilarsay eclarationsday, andyay adhay 
evernay ecantedray emthay.  Andyay, oremay anthay isthay, eythay acedplay inyay ethay 
atformplay orfay ymay acceptanceyay, andyay asyay ayay awlay otay emselvesthay andyay otay emay, 
ethay earclay andyay emphaticyay esolutionray ichwhay Iyay ownay eadray: 
 
"Esolvedray:  atthay ethay aintenancemay inviolateyay 
ofyay ethay ightsray ofyay ethay Atesstay, andyay especiallyyay 
ethay ightray ofyay eachyay Atestay otay orderyay andyay ontrolcay 
itsyay ownyay omesticday institutionsyay accordingyay otay 
itsyay ownyay udgmentjay exclusivelyyay, isyay essentialyay 
otay atthay alancebay ofyay owerpay onyay ichwhay ethay erfectionpay 
andyay enduranceyay ofyay ouryay oliticalpay abricfay ependday, 
andyay eway enounceday ethay awlesslay invasionyay ybay armedyay 
orcefay ofyay ethay oilsay ofyay anyyay Atestay oryay Erritorytay, 
onay attermay underyay atwhay etextpray, 
asyay amongyay ethay avestgray ofyay imescray." 
 
Iyay ownay eiterateray esethay entimentssay; andyay, inyay oingday osay, Iyay onlyyay esspray uponyay 
ethay ublicpay attentionyay ethay ostmay onclusivecay evidenceyay ofyay ichwhay ethay asecay 
isyay usceptiblesay, atthay ethay opertypray, eacepay, andyay ecuritysay ofyay onay ectionsay 
areyay otay ebay inyay anyyay iseway endangeredyay ybay ethay ownay incomingyay administrationyay. 
Iyay addyay, ootay, atthay allyay ethay otectionpray ichwhay, onsistentlycay ithway ethay 
Onstitutioncay andyay ethay awslay, ancay ebay ivengay, illway ebay eerfullychay ivengay 
otay allyay ethay Atesstay enwhay awfullylay emandedday, orfay ateverwhay ause--cay 
asyay eerfullychay otay oneyay ectionsay asyay otay anotheryay. 
 
Erethay isyay uchmay ontroversycay aboutyay ethay eliveringday upyay ofyay ugitivesfay 
omfray ervicesay oryay aborlay.  Ethay auseclay Iyay ownay eadray isyay asyay ainlyplay 
ittenwray inyay ethay Onstitutioncay asyay anyyay otheryay ofyay itsyay ovisionspray: 
 
"Onay ersonpay eldhay otay ervicesay oryay aborlay inyay oneyay Atestay, 
underyay ethay awslay ereofthay, escapingyay intoyay anotheryay, 
allshay inyay onsequencecay ofyay anyyay awlay oryay egulationray 
ereinthay ebay ischargedday omfray uchsay ervicesay oryay aborlay, 
utbay allshay ebay eliveredday upyay onyay aimclay ofyay ethay artypay 
otay omwhay uchsay ervicesay oryay aborlay aymay ebay ueday." 
 
Ityay isyay arcelyscay uestionedqay atthay isthay ovisionpray asway intendedyay ybay osethay 
owhay ademay ityay orfay ethay eclaimingray ofyay atwhay eway allcay ugitivefay avesslay; 
andyay ethay intentionyay ofyay ethay awgiverlay isyay ethay awlay.  Allyay embersmay 
ofyay Ongresscay earsway eirthay upportsay otay ethay olewhay Onstitution--cay 
otay isthay ovisionpray asyay uchmay asyay otay anyyay otheryay.  Otay ethay opositionpray, 
enthay, atthay avesslay osewhay asescay omecay ithinway ethay ermstay ofyay isthay auseclay 
"allshay ebay eliveredday upyay", eirthay oathsyay areyay unanimousyay.  Ownay, ifyay eythay 
ouldway akemay ethay effortyay inyay oodgay empertay, ouldcay eythay otnay ithway earlynay 
equalyay unanimityyay amefray andyay asspay ayay awlay ybay eansmay ofyay ichwhay otay eepkay oodgay 
atthay unanimousyay oathyay? 
 
Erethay isyay omesay ifferenceday ofyay opinionyay etherwhay isthay auseclay ouldshay 
ebay enforcedyay ybay ationalnay oryay ybay Atestay authorityyay; utbay urelysay atthay 
ifferenceday isyay otnay ayay eryvay aterialmay oneyay.  Ifyay ethay aveslay isyay otay ebay 
urrenderedsay, ityay ancay ebay ofyay utbay ittlelay onsequencecay otay imhay oryay otay othersyay 
ybay ichwhay authorityyay ityay isyay oneday.  Andyay ouldshay anyyay oneyay inyay anyyay asecay ebay 
ontentcay atthay ishay oathyay allshay ogay unkeptyay onyay ayay erelymay unsubstantialyay 
ontroversycay asyay otay OWHAY ityay allshay ebay eptkay? 
 
Againyay, inyay anyyay awlay uponyay isthay ubjectsay, oughtyay otnay allyay ethay afeguardssay ofyay 
ibertylay ownknay inyay ivilizedcay andyay umanehay urisprudencejay otay ebay introducedyay, 
osay atthay ayay eefray anmay ebay otnay, inyay anyyay asecay, urrenderedsay asyay ayay aveslay? 
Andyay ightmay ityay otnay ebay ellway atyay ethay amesay imetay otay ovidepray ybay awlay orfay ethay 
enforcementyay ofyay atthay auseclay inyay ethay Onstitutioncay ichwhay uaranteesgay atthay 
"ethay itizencay ofyay eachyay Atestay allshay ebay entitledyay otay allyay ivilegedpray andyay 
immunitiesyay ofyay itizenscay inyay ethay everalsay Atesstay?" 
 
Iyay aketay ethay officialyay oathyay odaytay ithway onay entalmay eservationsray, 
andyay ithway onay urposepay otay onstruecay ethay Onstitutioncay oryay awslay ybay 
anyyay ypercriticalhay ulesray.  Andyay ilewhay Iyay oday otnay oosechay ownay otay ecifyspay 
articularpay actsyay ofyay Ongresscay asyay operpray otay ebay enforcedyay, Iyay oday uggestsay 
atthay ityay illway ebay uchmay afersay orfay allyay, othbay inyay officialyay andyay ivatepray ationsstay, 
otay onformcay otay andyay abideyay ybay allyay osethay actsyay ichwhay andstay unrepealedyay, 
anthay otay iolatevay anyyay ofyay emthay, ustingtray otay indfay impunityyay inyay avinghay 
emthay eldhay otay ebay unConstitutionalyay. 
 
Ityay isyay eventy-twosay earsyay incesay ethay irstfay inaugurationyay ofyay ayay Esidentpray 
underyay ouryay ationalnay Onstitutioncay.  Uringday atthay eriodpay ifteenfay ifferentday 
andyay eatlygray istinguishedday itizenscay avehay, inyay uccessionsay, administeredyay 
ethay executiveyay anchbray ofyay ethay overnmentgay.  Eythay avehay onductedcay ityay oughthray 
anymay erilspay, andyay enerallygay ithway eatgray uccesssay.  Etyay, ithway allyay isthay opescay 
ofyay ecedentpray, Iyay ownay enteryay uponyay ethay amesay asktay orfay ethay iefbray Onstitutionalcay 
ermtay ofyay ourfay earsyay underyay eatgray andyay eculiarpay ifficultyday.  Ayay isruptionday ofyay 
ethay Ederalfay Unionyay, eretoforehay onlyyay enacedmay, isyay ownay ormidablyfay attemptedyay. 
 
Iyay oldhay atthay, inyay ontemplationcay ofyay universalyay awlay andyay ofyay ethay Onstitutioncay, 
ethay Unionyay ofyay esethay Atesstay isyay erpetualpay.  Erpetuitypay isyay impliedyay, 
ifyay otnay expressedyay, inyay ethay undamentalfay awlay ofyay allyay ationalnay overnmentsgay. 
Ityay isyay afesay otay assertyay atthay onay overnmentgay operpray everyay adhay ayay ovisionpray 
inyay itsyay organicyay awlay orfay itsyay ownyay erminationtay.  Ontinuecay otay executeyay allyay 
ethay expressyay ovisionspray ofyay ouryay Ationalnay Onstitutioncay, andyay ethay Unionyay illway 
endureyay orever--itfay eingbay impossibleyay otay estroyday ityay exceptyay ybay omesay actionyay 
otnay ovidedpray orfay inyay ethay instrumentyay itselfyay. 
 
Againyay, ifyay ethay Unitedyay Atesstay ebay otnay ayay overnmentgay operpray, utbay anyay associationyay 
ofyay Atesstay inyay ethay aturenay ofyay ontractcay erelymay, ancay ityay, asyay ayay ontractcay, 
ebay eaceablypay unmadeyay ybay esslay anthay allyay ethay artiespay owhay ademay ityay? 
Oneyay artypay otay ayay ontractcay aymay iolatevay it--breakyay ityay, osay otay eakspay; 
utbay oesday ityay otnay equireray allyay otay awfullylay escindray ityay? 
 
Escendingday omfray esethay eneralgay inciplespray, eway indfay ethay opositionpray 
atthay inyay egallay ontemplationcay ethay Unionyay isyay erpetualpay onfirmedcay ybay 
ethay istoryhay ofyay ethay Unionyay itselfyay.  Ethay Unionyay isyay uchmay olderyay anthay 
ethay Onstitutioncay.  Ityay asway ormedfay, inyay actfay, ybay ethay Articlesyay ofyay 
Associationyay inyay 1774.  Ityay asway aturedmay andyay ontinuedcay ybay ethay 
Eclarationday ofyay Independenceyay inyay 1776.  Ityay asway urtherfay aturedmay, 
andyay ethay aithfay ofyay allyay ethay enthay irteenthay Atesstay expresslyyay ightedplay 
andyay engagedyay atthay ityay ouldshay ebay erpetualpay, ybay ethay Articlesyay ofyay Onfederationcay 
inyay 1778.  Andyay, inallyfay, inyay 1787 oneyay ofyay ethay eclaredday objectsyay orfay ordainingyay 
andyay establishingyay ethay Onstitutioncay asway "OTAY ORMFAY Ayay OREMAY ERFECTPAY NIONUAY." 
 
Utbay ifyay ethay estructionday ofyay ethay Unionyay ybay oneyay oryay ybay ayay artpay onlyyay ofyay ethay Atesstay 
ebay awfullylay ossiblepay, ethay Unionyay isyay ESSLAY erfectpay anthay eforebay ethay Onstitutioncay, 
avinghay ostlay ethay italvay elementyay ofyay erpetuitypay. 
 
Ityay ollowsfay omfray esethay iewsvay atthay onay Atestay uponyay itsyay ownyay eremay otionmay 
ancay awfullylay etgay outyay ofyay ethay Unionyay; atthay Esolvesray andyay Ordinancesyay 
otay atthay effectyay areyay egallylay oidvay; andyay atthay actsyay ofyay iolencevay, 
ithinway anyyay Atestay oryay Atesstay, againstyay ethay authorityyay ofyay ethay Unitedyay Atesstay, 
areyay insurrectionaryyay oryay evolutionaryray, accordingyay otay ircumstancescay. 
 
Iyay ereforethay onsidercay atthay, inyay iewvay ofyay ethay Onstitutioncay andyay ethay awslay, 
ethay Unionyay isyay unbrokenyay; andyay otay ethay extentyay ofyay ymay abilityyay Iyay allshay aketay arecay, 
asyay ethay Onstitutioncay itselfyay expresslyyay enjoinsyay uponyay emay, atthay ethay 
awslay ofyay ethay Unionyay ebay aithfullyfay executedyay inyay allyay ethay Atesstay. 
Oingday isthay Iyay eemday otay ebay onlyyay ayay implesay utyday onyay ymay artpay; 
andyay Iyay allshay erformpay ityay osay arfay asyay acticablepray, unlessyay ymay 
ightfulray astersmay, ethay Americanyay eoplepay, allshay ithholdway ethay 
equisiteray eansmay, oryay inyay omesay authoritativeyay annermay irectday ethay ontrarycay. 
Iyay usttray isthay illway otnay ebay egardedray asyay ayay enacemay, utbay onlyyay asyay ethay 
eclaredday urposepay ofyay ethay Unionyay atthay ityay ILLWAY Onstitutionallycay 
efendday andyay aintainmay itselfyay. 
 
Inyay oingday isthay erethay eedsnay otay ebay onay oodshedblay oryay iolencevay; andyay erethay 
allshay ebay onenay, unlessyay ityay ebay orcedfay uponyay ethay ationalnay authorityyay. 
Ethay owerpay onfidedcay otay emay illway ebay usedyay otay oldhay, occupyyay, andyay ossesspay 
ethay opertypray andyay acesplay elongingbay otay ethay overnmentgay, andyay otay ollectcay 
ethay utiesday andyay impostsyay; utbay eyondbay atwhay aymay ebay ecessarynay orfay esethay objectsyay, 
erethay illway ebay onay invasionyay, onay usingyay ofyay orcefay againstyay oryay amongyay ethay eoplepay 
anywhereyay.  Erewhay ostilityhay otay ethay Unitedyay Atesstay, inyay anyyay interioryay ocalitylay, 
allshay ebay osay eatgray andyay universalyay asyay otay eventpray ompetentcay esidentray itizenscay 
omfray oldinghay ethay Ederalfay officesyay, erethay illway ebay onay attemptyay otay orcefay 
obnoxiousyay angersstray amongyay ethay eoplepay orfay atthay objectyay.  Ilewhay ethay ictstray 
egallay ightray aymay existyay inyay ethay overnmentgay otay enforceyay ethay exerciseyay ofyay 
esethay officesyay, ethay attemptyay otay oday osay ouldway ebay osay irritatingyay, 
andyay osay earlynay impracticableyay ithalway, atthay Iyay eemday ityay etterbay 
otay oregofay orfay ethay imetay ethay usesyay ofyay uchsay officesyay. 
 
Ethay ailsmay, unlessyay epelledray, illway ontinuecay otay ebay urnishedfay inyay allyay artspay 
ofyay ethay Unionyay.  Osay arfay asyay ossiblepay, ethay eoplepay everywhereyay allshay avehay atthay 
ensesay ofyay erfectpay ecuritysay ichwhay isyay ostmay avorablefay otay almcay oughtthay 
andyay eflectionray.  Ethay oursecay erehay indicatedyay illway ebay ollowedfay unlessyay urrentcay 
eventsyay andyay experienceyay allshay owshay ayay odificationmay oryay angechay otay ebay operpray, 
andyay inyay everyyay asecay andyay exigencyyay ymay estbay iscretionday illway ebay exercisedyay 
accordingyay otay ircumstancescay actuallyyay existingyay, andyay ithway ayay iewvay andyay 
ayay opehay ofyay ayay eacefulpay olutionsay ofyay ethay ationalnay oublestray andyay ethay 
estorationray ofyay aternalfray ympathiessay andyay affectionsyay. 
 
Atthay erethay areyay ersonspay inyay oneyay ectionsay oryay anotheryay owhay eeksay otay estroyday 
ethay Unionyay atyay allyay eventsyay, andyay areyay adglay ofyay anyyay etextpray otay oday ityay, Iyay illway 
eithernay affirmyay ornay enyday; utbay ifyay erethay ebay uchsay, Iyay eednay addressyay onay ordway 
otay emthay.  Otay osethay, oweverhay, owhay eallyray ovelay ethay Unionyay aymay Iyay otnay eakspay? 
 
Eforebay enteringyay uponyay osay avegray ayay attermay asyay ethay estructionday ofyay ouryay 
ationalnay abricfay, ithway allyay itsyay enefitsbay, itsyay emoriesmay, andyay itsyay opeshay, 
ouldway ityay otnay ebay iseway otay ascertainyay eciselypray ywhay eway oday ityay? 
Illway ouyay azardhay osay esperateday ayay epstay ilewhay erethay isyay anyyay ossibilitypay 
atthay anyyay ortionpay ofyay ethay illsyay ouyay yflay omfray avehay onay ealray existenceyay? 
Illway ouyay, ilewhay ethay ertaincay illsyay ouyay yflay otay areyay eatergray anthay allyay 
ethay ealray onesyay ouyay yflay om--willfray ouyay iskray ethay ommissioncay ofyay osay 
earfulfay ayay istakemay? 
 
Allyay ofesspray otay ebay ontentcay inyay ethay Unionyay ifyay allyay Onstitutionalcay ightsray 
ancay ebay aintainedmay.  Isyay ityay uetray, enthay, atthay anyyay ightray, ainlyplay ittenwray 
inyay ethay Onstitutioncay, ashay eenbay eniedday?  Iyay inkthay otnay.  Appilyhay ethay umanhay 
indmay isyay osay onstitutedcay atthay onay artypay ancay eachray otay ethay audacityyay ofyay oingday isthay. 
Inkthay, ifyay ouyay ancay, ofyay ayay inglesay instanceyay inyay ichwhay ayay ainlyplay ittenwray ovisionpray 
ofyay ethay Onstitutioncay ashay everyay eenbay eniedday.  Ifyay ybay ethay eremay orcefay ofyay umbersnay ayay 
ajoritymay ouldshay epriveday ayay inoritymay ofyay anyyay earlyclay ittenwray Onstitutionalcay ightray, 
ityay ightmay, inyay ayay oralmay ointpay ofyay iewvay, ustifyjay evolution--certainlyray ouldway ifyay uchsay 
ayay ightray ereway ayay italvay oneyay.  Utbay uchsay isyay otnay ouryay asecay.  Allyay ethay italvay ightsray ofyay 
inoritiesmay andyay ofyay individualsyay areyay osay ainlyplay assuredyay otay emthay ybay affirmationsyay 
andyay egationsnay, uarantiesgay andyay ohibitionspray, inyay ethay Onstitutioncay, atthay 
ontroversiescay evernay ariseyay oncerningcay emthay.  Utbay onay organicyay awlay ancay everyay ebay 
amedfray ithway ayay ovisionpray ecificallyspay applicableyay otay everyyay uestionqay ichwhay aymay 
occuryay inyay acticalpray administrationyay.  Onay oresightfay ancay anticipateyay, 
ornay anyyay ocumentday ofyay easonableray engthlay ontaincay, expressyay ovisionspray 
orfay allyay ossiblepay uestionsqay.  Allshay ugitivesfay omfray aborlay ebay urrenderedsay 
ybay ationalnay oryay Atestay authorityyay?  Ethay Onstitutioncay oesday otnay expresslyyay aysay. 
Aymay Ongresscay ohibitpray averyslay inyay ethay Erritoriestay?  Ethay Onstitutioncay oesday otnay 
expresslyyay aysay.  USTMAY Ongresscay otectpray averyslay inyay ethay Erritoriestay? 
Ethay Onstitutioncay oesday otnay expresslyyay aysay. 
 
Omfray uestionsqay ofyay isthay assclay ingspray allyay ouryay onstitutionalcay ontroversiescay, 
andyay eway ivideday uponyay emthay intoyay ajoritiesmay andyay inoritiesmay.  Ifyay ethay inoritymay 
illway otnay acquiesceyay, ethay ajoritymay ustmay, oryay ethay overnmentgay ustmay easecay. 
Erethay isyay onay otheryay alternativeyay; orfay ontinuingcay ethay overnmentgay isyay 
acquiescenceyay onyay oneyay idesay oryay ethay otheryay. 
 
Ifyay ayay inoritymay inyay uchsay asecay illway ecedesay atherray anthay acquiesceyay, 
eythay akemay ayay ecedentpray ichwhay inyay urntay illway ivideday andyay uinray emthay; 
orfay ayay inoritymay ofyay eirthay ownyay illway ecedesay omfray emthay eneverwhay 
ayay ajoritymay efusesray otay ebay ontrolledcay ybay uchsay inoritymay. 
Orfay instanceyay, ywhay aymay otnay anyyay ortionpay ofyay ayay ewnay 
onfederacycay ayay earyay oryay otway encehay arbitrarilyyay ecedesay againyay, 
eciselypray asyay ortionspay ofyay ethay esentpray Unionyay ownay aimclay otay ecedesay omfray ityay? 
Allyay owhay erishchay isunionday entimentssay areyay ownay eingbay educatedyay otay ethay 
exactyay empertay ofyay oingday isthay. 
 
Isyay erethay uchsay erfectpay identityyay ofyay interestsyay amongyay ethay Atesstay 
otay omposecay ayay ewnay Unionyay, asyay otay oducepray armonyhay onlyyay, 
andyay eventpray enewedray ecessionsay? 
 
Ainlyplay, ethay entralcay ideayay ofyay ecessionsay isyay ethay essenceyay ofyay anarchyyay. 
Ayay ajoritymay eldhay inyay estraintray ybay onstitutionalcay eckschay andyay imitationslay, 
andyay alwaysyay angingchay easilyyay ithway eliberateday angeschay ofyay opularpay 
opinionsyay andyay entimentssay, isyay ethay onlyyay uetray overeignsay ofyay ayay eefray eoplepay. 
Oeverwhay ejectsray ityay oesday, ofyay ecessitynay, yflay otay anarchyyay oryay otay espotismday. 
Unanimityyay isyay impossibleyay; ethay uleray ofyay ayay inoritymay, asyay ayay ermanentpay arrangementyay, 
isyay ollywhay inadmissibleyay; osay atthay, ejectingray ethay ajoritymay inciplepray, 
anarchyyay oryay espotismday inyay omesay ormfay isyay allyay atthay isyay eftlay. 
 
Iyay oday otnay orgetfay ethay ositionpay, assumedyay ybay omesay, atthay Onstitutionalcay 
uestionsqay areyay otay ebay ecidedday ybay ethay Upremesay Ourtcay; ornay oday Iyay enyday 
atthay uchsay ecisionsday ustmay ebay indingbay, inyay anyyay asecay, uponyay ethay artiespay 
otay ayay uitsay, asyay otay ethay objectyay ofyay atthay uitsay, ilewhay eythay areyay alsoyay entitledyay 
otay eryvay ighhay espectray andyay onsiderationcay inyay allyay arallelpay asescay ybay allyay otheryay 
epartmentsday ofyay ethay overnmentgay.  Andyay ilewhay ityay isyay obviouslyyay ossiblepay atthay 
uchsay ecisionday aymay ebay erroneousyay inyay anyyay ivengay asecay, illstay ethay evilyay effectyay 
ollowingfay ityay, eingbay imitedlay otay atthay articularpay asecay, ithway ethay ancechay atthay 
ityay aymay ebay overruledyay andyay evernay ecomebay ayay ecedentpray orfay otheryay asescay, 
ancay etterbay ebay ornebay anthay ouldcay ethay evilsyay ofyay ayay ifferentday acticepray. 
Atyay ethay amesay imetay, ethay andidcay itizencay ustmay onfesscay atthay ifyay ethay olicypay 
ofyay ethay overnmentgay, uponyay italvay uestionsqay affectingyay ethay olewhay eoplepay, 
isyay otay ebay irrevocablyyay ixedfay ybay ecisionsday ofyay ethay Upremesay Ourtcay, 
ethay instantyay eythay areyay ademay, inyay ordinaryyay itigationlay etweenbay artiespay 
inyay ersonalpay actionsyay, ethay eoplepay illway avehay easedcay otay ebay eirthay ownyay ulersray, 
avinghay otay atthay extentyay acticallypray esignedray eirthay overnmentgay intoyay ethay andshay 
ofyay atthay eminentyay ibunaltray.  Ornay isyay erethay inyay isthay iewvay anyyay assaultyay uponyay 
ethay ourtcay oryay ethay udgesjay.  Ityay isyay ayay utyday omfray ichwhay eythay aymay otnay inkshray 
otay ecideday asescay operlypray oughtbray eforebay emthay, andyay ityay isyay onay aultfay ofyay 
eirsthay ifyay othersyay eeksay otay urntay eirthay ecisionsday otay oliticalpay urposespay. 
 
Oneyay ectionsay ofyay ouryay ountrycay elievesbay averyslay isyay IGHTRAY, andyay oughtyay 
otay ebay extendedyay, ilewhay ethay otheryay elievesbay ityay isyay RONGWAY, andyay oughtyay 
otnay otay ebay extendedyay.  Isthay isyay ethay onlyyay ubstantialsay isputeday. 
Ethay ugitive-slavefay auseclay ofyay ethay Onstitutioncay, andyay ethay awlay orfay ethay 
uppressionsay ofyay ethay oreignfay ave-tradeslay, areyay eachyay asyay ellway enforcedyay, 
erhapspay, asyay anyyay awlay ancay everyay ebay inyay ayay ommunitycay erewhay ethay oralmay 
ensesay ofyay ethay eoplepay imperfectlyyay upportssay ethay awlay itselfyay. 
Ethay eatgray odybay ofyay ethay eoplepay abideyay ybay ethay ydray egallay obligationyay 
inyay othbay asescay, andyay ayay ewfay eakbray overyay inyay eachyay.  Isthay, Iyay inkthay, 
annotcay ebay erfectlypay uredcay; andyay ityay ouldway ebay orseway inyay othbay asescay 
FTERAAY ethay eparationsay ofyay ethay ectionssay anthay EFOREBAY.  Ethay oreignfay 
ave-tradeslay, ownay imperfectlyyay uppressedsay, ouldway ebay ultimatelyyay evivedray, 
ithoutway estrictionray, inyay oneyay ectionsay, ilewhay ugitivefay avesslay, 
ownay onlyyay artiallypay urrenderedsay, ouldway otnay ebay urrenderedsay 
atyay allyay ybay ethay otheryay. 
 
Ysicallyphay eakingspay, eway annotcay eparatesay.  Eway annotcay emoveray ouryay 
espectiveray ectionssay omfray eachyay otheryay, ornay uildbay anyay impassableyay allway 
etweenbay emthay.  Ayay usbandhay andyay ifeway aymay ebay ivorcedday, andyay ogay outyay ofyay 
ethay esencepray andyay eyondbay ethay eachray ofyay eachyay otheryay; utbay ethay ifferentday 
artspay ofyay ouryay ountrycay annotcay oday isthay.  Eythay annotcay utbay emainray 
acefay otay acefay, andyay intercourseyay, eitheryay amicableyay oryay ostilehay, 
ustmay ontinuecay etweenbay emthay.  Isyay ityay ossiblepay, enthay, otay akemay 
atthay intercourseyay oremay advantageousyay oryay oremay atisfactorysay afteryay 
eparationsay anthay eforebay?  Ancay aliensyay akemay eatiestray easieryay anthay 
iendsfray ancay akemay awslay?  Ancay eatiestray ebay oremay aithfullyfay enforcedyay 
etweenbay aliensyay anthay awslay ancay amongyay iendsfray?  Upposesay ouyay ogay otay arway, 
ouyay annotcay ightfay alwaysyay; andyay enwhay, afteryay uchmay osslay onyay othbay idessay, 
anyay onay aingay onyay eitheryay, ouyay easecay ightingfay, ethay identicalyay oldyay uestionsqay 
asyay otay ermstay ofyay intercourseyay areyay againyay uponyay ouyay. 
 
Isthay ountrycay, ithway itsyay institutionsyay, elongsbay otay ethay eoplepay owhay inhabityay ityay. 
Eneverwhay eythay allshay owgray earyway ofyay ethay existingyay overnmentgay, eythay ancay exerciseyay 
eirthay ONSTITUTIONALCAY ightray ofyay amendingyay ityay, oryay eirthay EVOLUTIONARYRAY ightray 
otay ismemberday oryay overthrowyay ityay.  Iyay annotcay ebay ignorantyay ofyay ethay actfay 
atthay anymay orthyway andyay atrioticpay itizenscay areyay esirousday ofyay avinghay ethay 
ationalnay Onstitutioncay amendedyay.  Ilewhay Iyay akemay onay ecommendationray ofyay 
amendmentsyay, Iyay ullyfay ecognizeray ethay ightfulray authorityyay ofyay ethay eoplepay 
overyay ethay olewhay ubjectsay, otay ebay exercisedyay inyay eitheryay ofyay ethay odesmay escribedpray 
inyay ethay instrumentyay itselfyay; andyay Iyay ouldshay, underyay existingyay ircumstancescay, 
avorfay atherray anthay opposeyay ayay airfay opportunityyay eingbay affordedyay ethay eoplepay 
otay actyay uponyay ityay.  Iyay illway enturevay otay addyay atthay otay emay ethay onventioncay odemay 
eemssay eferablepray, inyay atthay ityay allowsyay amendmentsyay otay originateyay ithway 
ethay eoplepay emselvesthay, insteadyay ofyay onlyyay ermittingpay emthay otay aketay oryay 
ejectray opositionspray originatedyay ybay othersyay otnay especiallyyay osenchay 
orfay ethay urposepay, andyay ichwhay ightmay otnay ebay eciselypray uchsay asyay eythay ouldway 
ishway otay eitheryay acceptyay oryay efuseray.  Iyay understandyay ayay oposedpray amendmentyay 
otay ethay Onstitution--whichcay amendmentyay, oweverhay, Iyay avehay otnay een--hassay 
assedpay Ongresscay, otay ethay effectyay atthay ethay Ederalfay Overnmentgay allshay 
evernay interfereyay ithway ethay omesticday institutionsyay ofyay ethay Atesstay, 
includingyay atthay ofyay ersonspay eldhay otay ervicesay.  Otay avoidyay isconstructionmay 
ofyay atwhay Iyay avehay aidsay, Iyay epartday omfray ymay urposepay otnay otay eakspay ofyay articularpay 
amendmentsyay osay arfay asyay otay aysay atthay, oldinghay uchsay ayay ovisionpray otay ownay ebay 
impliedyay Onstitutionalcay awlay, Iyay avehay onay objectionyay otay itsyay eingbay ademay expressyay 
andyay irrevocableyay. 
 
Ethay iefchay agistratemay erivesday allyay ishay authorityyay omfray ethay eoplepay, 
andyay eythay avehay onferredcay onenay uponyay imhay otay ixfay ermstay orfay ethay 
eparationsay ofyay ethay atesstay.  Ethay eoplepay emselvesthay ancay oday isthay 
alsoyay ifyay eythay oosechay; utbay ethay executiveyay, asyay uchsay, ashay othingnay otay 
oday ithway ityay.  Ishay utyday isyay otay administeryay ethay esentpray overnmentgay, 
asyay ityay amecay otay ishay andshay, andyay otay ansmittray ityay, unimpairedyay ybay imhay, 
otay ishay uccessorsay. 
 
Ywhay ouldshay erethay otnay ebay ayay atientpay onfidencecay inyay ethay ultimateyay usticejay 
ofyay ethay eoplepay?  Isyay erethay anyyay etterbay oryay equalyay opehay inyay ethay orldway? 
Inyay ouryay esentpray ifferencesday isyay eitheryay artypay ithoutway aithfay ofyay eingbay 
inyay ethay ightray?  Ifyay ethay Almightyyay Ulerray ofyay Ationsnay, ithway ishay eternalyay 
uthtray andyay usticejay, ebay onyay ouryay idesay ofyay ethay Orthnay, oryay onyay oursyay 
ofyay ethay Outhsay, atthay uthtray andyay atthay usticejay illway urelysay evailpray, 
ybay ethay udgmentjay ofyay isthay eatgray ibunaltray, ethay Americanyay eoplepay. 
 
Ybay ethay amefray ofyay ethay overnmentgay underyay ichwhay eway ivelay, isthay amesay eoplepay 
avehay iselyway ivengay eirthay ublicpay ervantssay utbay ittlelay owerpay orfay ischiefmay; 
andyay avehay, ithway equalyay isdomway, ovidedpray orfay ethay eturnray ofyay atthay ittlelay 
otay eirthay ownyay andshay atyay eryvay ortshay intervalsyay.  Ilewhay ethay eoplepay etainray 
eirthay irtuevay andyay igilancevay, onay administrationyay, ybay anyyay extremeyay ofyay 
ickednessway oryay ollyfay, ancay eryvay eriouslysay injureyay ethay overnmentgay 
inyay ethay ortshay acespay ofyay ourfay earsyay. 
 
Ymay ountrymencay, oneyay andyay allyay, inkthay almlycay andyay ELLWAY uponyay isthay 
olewhay ubjectsay.  Othingnay aluablevay ancay ebay ostlay ybay akingtay imetay. 
Ifyay erethay ebay anyay objectyay otay URRYHAY anyyay ofyay ouyay inyay othay astehay otay ayay epstay 
ichwhay ouyay ouldway evernay aketay ELIBERATELYDAY, atthay objectyay illway ebay 
ustratedfray ybay akingtay imetay; utbay onay oodgay objectyay ancay ebay ustratedfray 
ybay ityay.  Uchsay ofyay ouyay asyay areyay ownay issatisfiedday, illstay avehay ethay 
oldyay Onstitutioncay unimpairedyay, andyay, onyay ethay ensitivesay ointpay, 
ethay awslay ofyay ouryay ownyay amingfray underyay ityay; ilewhay ethay ewnay administrationyay 
illway avehay onay immediateyay owerpay, ifyay ityay ouldway, otay angechay eitheryay. 
Ifyay ityay ereway admittedyay atthay ouyay owhay areyay issatisfiedday oldhay ethay 
ightray idesay inyay ethay isputeday, erethay illstay isyay onay inglesay oodgay easonray 
orfay ecipitatepray actionyay.  Intelligenceyay, atriotismpay, Istianitychray, 
andyay ayay irmfay elianceray onyay imhay owhay ashay evernay etyay orsakenfay isthay avoredfay andlay, 
areyay illstay ompetentcay otay adjustyay inyay ethay estbay ayway allyay ouryay esentpray ifficultyday.

Ethay Eclarationday ofyay Independenceyay ofyay Ethay Unitedyay Atesstay ofyay Americayay 
 
 
 
 
NIAY ONGRESSCAY, Ulyjay 4, 1776 
 
Ethay unanimousyay Eclarationday ofyay ethay irteenthay unitedyay Atesstay ofyay Americayay 
 
 
 
Enwhay inyay ethay Oursecay ofyay umanhay eventsyay, ityay ecomesbay ecessarynay orfay 
oneyay eoplepay otay issolveday ethay oliticalpay andsbay ichwhay avehay onnectedcay 
emthay ithway anotheryay, andyay otay assumeyay, amongyay ethay Owerspay ofyay ethay earthyay, 
ethay eparatesay andyay equalyay ationstay otay ichwhay ethay Awslay ofyay Aturenay andyay 
ofyay Ature'snay Odgay entitleyay emthay, ayay ecentday espectray otay ethay opinionsyay 
ofyay ankindmay equiresray atthay eythay ouldshay eclareday ethay ausescay ichwhay 
impelyay emthay otay ethay eparationsay. 
 
Eway oldhay esethay uthstray otay ebay elf-evidentsay, atthay allyay enmay areyay eatedcray equalyay, 
atthay eythay areyay endowedyay ybay eirthay Eatorcray ithway ertaincay unalienableyay Ightsray, 
atthay amongyay esethay areyay Ifelay, Ibertylay, andyay ethay ursuitpay ofyay Appinesshay. 
Atthay otay ecuresay esethay ightsray, Overnmentsgay areyay institutedyay amongyay Enmay, 
erivingday eirthay ustjay owerspay omfray ethay onsentcay ofyay ethay overnedgay, 
Atthay eneverwhay anyyay Ormfay ofyay Overnmentgay ecomesbay estructiveday ofyay esethay endsyay, 
ityay isyay ethay Ightray ofyay ethay Eoplepay otay alteryay oryay otay abolishyay ityay, andyay otay instituteyay 
ewnay Overnmentgay, ayinglay itsyay oundationfay onyay uchsay inciplespray andyay organizingyay 
itsyay owerspay inyay uchsay ormfay, asyay otay emthay allshay eemsay ostmay ikelylay otay effectyay 
eirthay Afetysay andyay Appinesshay.  Udencepray, indeedyay, illway ictateday atthay Overnmentsgay 
onglay establishedyay ouldshay otnay ebay angedchay orfay ightlay andyay ansienttray ausescay; 
andyay accordinglyyay allyay experienceyay athhay ownshay, atthay ankindmay areyay oremay isposedday 
otay uffersay, ilewhay evilsyay areyay ufferablesay, anthay otay ightray emselvesthay ybay abolishingyay 
ethay ormsfay otay ichwhay eythay areyay accustomedyay.  Utbay enwhay ayay onglay aintray ofyay abusesyay andyay 
usurpationsyay, ursuingpay invariablyyay ethay amesay Objectyay evincesyay ayay esignday otay educeray 
emthay underyay absoluteyay Espotismday, ityay isyay eirthay ightray, ityay isyay eirthay utyday, otay owthray 
offyay uchsay Overnmentgay, andyay otay ovidepray ewnay Uardsgay orfay eirthay uturefay ecuritysay. 
uch--Say ashay eenbay ethay atientpay ufferancesay ofyay esethay Oloniescay; andyay uchsay isyay ownay 
ethay ecessitynay ichwhay onstrainscay emthay otay alteryay eirthay ormerfay Ystemssay ofyay Overnmentgay. 
Ethay istoryhay ofyay ethay esentpray Ingkay ofyay Eatgray Itainbray isyay ayay istoryhay ofyay epeatedray 
injuriesyay andyay usurpationsyay, allyay avinghay inyay irectday objectyay ethay establishmentyay 
ofyay anyay absoluteyay Yrannytay overyay esethay Atesstay.  Otay ovepray isthay, etlay Actsfay 
ebay ubmittedsay otay ayay andidcay orldway. 
 
Ehay ashay efusedray ishay Assentyay otay Awslay, ethay ostmay olesomewhay andyay ecessarynay 
orfay ethay ublicpay oodgay. 
 
Ehay ashay orbiddenfay ishay Overnorsgay otay asspay Awslay ofyay immediateyay 
andyay essingpray importanceyay, unlessyay uspendedsay inyay eirthay operationyay 
illtay ishay Assentyay ouldshay ebay obtainedyay; andyay enwhay osay uspendedsay, 
ehay ashay utterlyyay eglectednay otay attendyay otay emthay. 
 
Ehay ashay efusedray otay asspay otheryay Awslay orfay ethay accommodationyay ofyay 
argelay istrictsday ofyay eoplepay, unlessyay osethay eoplepay ouldway elinquishray 
ethay ightray ofyay Epresentationray inyay ethay Egislaturelay, ayay ightray 
inestimableyay otay emthay andyay ormidablefay otay yrantstay onlyyay. 
 
Ehay ashay alledcay ogethertay egislativelay odiesbay atyay acesplay unusualyay, 
uncomfortableyay, andyay istantday omfray ethay epositoryday ofyay eirthay 
Ublicpay Ecordsray, orfay ethay olesay urposepay ofyay atiguingfay emthay 
intoyay ompliancecay ithway ishay easuresmay. 
 
Ehay ashay issolvedday Epresentativeray Ouseshay epeatedlyray, orfay opposingyay 
ithway anlymay irmnessfay ishay invasionsyay onyay ethay ightsray ofyay ethay eoplepay. 
 
Ehay ashay efusedray orfay ayay onglay imetay, afteryay uchsay issolutionsday, 
otay ausecay othersyay otay ebay electedyay; erebywhay ethay Egislativelay Owerspay, 
incapableyay ofyay Annihilationyay, avehay eturnedray otay ethay Eoplepay atyay argelay 
orfay eirthay exerciseyay; ethay Atestay emainingray inyay ethay eanmay imetay exposedyay 
otay allyay ethay angersday ofyay invasionyay omfray ithoutway, andyay onvulsionscay ithinway. 
 
Ehay ashay endeavouredyay otay eventpray ethay opulationpay ofyay esethay Atesstay; 
orfay atthay urposepay obstructingyay ethay Awslay ofyay Aturalizationnay ofyay Oreignersfay; 
efusingray otay asspay othersyay otay encourageyay eirthay igrationmay itherhay, 
andyay aisingray ethay onditionscay ofyay ewnay Appropriationsyay ofyay Andslay. 
 
Ehay ashay obstructedyay ethay Administrationyay ofyay Usticejay, ybay efusingray ishay Assentyay 
otay Awslay orfay establishingyay Udiciaryjay Owerspay. 
 
Ehay ashay ademay udgesjay ependentday onyay ishay Illway aloneyay, orfay ethay enuretay 
ofyay eirthay officesyay, andyay ethay amountyay andyay aymentpay ofyay eirthay alariessay. 
 
Ehay ashay erectedyay ayay ultitudemay ofyay Ewnay Officesyay, andyay entsay itherhay armssway ofyay 
Officersyay otay arasshay ouryay Eoplepay, andyay eatyay outyay eirthay ubstancesay. 
 
Ehay ashay eptkay amongyay usyay, inyay imestay ofyay eacepay, Andingstay Armiesyay 
ithoutway ethay Onsentcay ofyay ouryay egislatureslay. 
 
Ehay ashay affectedyay otay enderray ethay Ilitarymay independentyay ofyay 
andyay uperiorsay otay ethay Ivilcay Owerpay. 
 
Ehay ashay ombinedcay ithway othersyay otay ubjectsay usyay otay ayay urisdictionjay 
oreignfay otay ouryay onstitutioncay, andyay unacknowledgedyay ybay ouryay awslay; 
ivinggay ishay Assentyay otay eirthay Actsyay ofyay etendedpray egislationlay: 
 
Orfay uarteringqay argelay odiesbay ofyay armedyay oopstray amongyay usyay: 
 
Orfay otectingpray emthay, ybay ayay ockmay Ialtray, omfray Unishmentpay orfay anyyay Urdersmay 
ichwhay eythay ouldshay ommitcay onyay ethay Inhabitantsyay ofyay esethay Atesstay: 
 
Orfay uttingcay offyay ouryay Adetray ithway allyay artspay ofyay ethay orldway: 
 
Orfay imposingyay axestay onyay usyay ithoutway ouryay Onsentcay: 
 
Orfay eprivingday usyay, inyay anymay asescay, ofyay ethay enefitsbay ofyay Ialtray ybay Uryjay: 
 
Orfay ansportingtray usyay eyondbay Eassay otay ebay iedtray orfay etendedpray offencesyay: 
 
Orfay abolishingyay ethay eefray Ystemsay ofyay Englishyay Awslay inyay ayay eighbouringnay 
Ovincepray, establishingyay ereinthay anyay Arbitraryyay overnmentgay, 
andyay enlargingyay itsyay Oundariesbay osay asyay otay enderray ityay atyay onceyay 
anyay exampleyay andyay itfay instrumentyay orfay introducingyay ethay amesay 
absoluteyay uleray intoyay esethay Oloniescay: 
 
Orfay akingtay awayyay ouryay Arterschay, abolishingyay ouryay ostmay aluablevay Awslay, 
andyay alteringyay undamentallyfay ethay Ormsfay ofyay ouryay Overnmentsgay: 
 
Orfay uspendingsay ouryay ownyay Egislatureslay, andyay eclaringday emselvesthay 
investedyay ithway Owerpay otay egislatelay orfay usyay inyay allyay asescay atsoeverwhay. 
 
Ehay ashay abdicatedyay Overnmentgay erehay, ybay eclaringday usyay outyay ofyay ishay Otectionpray 
andyay agingway Arway againstyay usyay. 
 
Ehay ashay underedplay ouryay eassay, avagedray ouryay Oastscay, urntbay ouryay ownstay, 
andyay estroyedday ethay iveslay ofyay ouryay eoplepay. 
 
Ehay isyay atyay isthay imetay ansportingtray argelay armiesyay ofyay oreignfay ercenariesmay 
otay ompleatcay ethay orksway ofyay eathday, esolationday andyay yrannytay, alreadyyay egunbay 
ithway ircumstancescay ofyay Ueltycray & erfidypay arcelyscay aralleledpay inyay ethay 
ostmay arbarousbay agesyay, andyay otallytay unworthyyay ofyay ethay Eadhay ofyay ayay ivilizedcay ationnay. 
 
Ehay ashay onstrainedcay ouryay ellowfay Itizenscay akentay Aptivecay onyay ethay ighhay Eassay 
otay earbay Armsyay againstyay eirthay Ountrycay, otay ecomebay ethay executionersyay ofyay 
eirthay iendsfray andyay Ethrenbray, oryay otay allfay emselvesthay ybay eirthay Andshay. 
 
Ehay ashay excitedyay omesticday insurrectionsyay amongstyay usyay, andyay ashay 
endeavouredyay otay ingbray onyay ethay inhabitantsyay ofyay ouryay ontiersfray, 
ethay ercilessmay Indianyay Avagessay, osewhay ownknay uleray ofyay arfareway, 
isyay anyay undistinguishedyay estructionday ofyay allyay agesyay, exessay andyay onditionscay. 
 
Inyay everyyay agestay ofyay esethay Oppressionsyay Eway avehay Etitionedpay orfay Edressray 
inyay ethay ostmay umblehay ermstay:  Ouryay epeatedray Etitionspay avehay eenbay answeredyay 
onlyyay ybay epeatedray injuryyay.  Ayay Incepray, osewhay aracterchay isyay usthay arkedmay 
ybay everyyay actyay ichwhay aymay efineday ayay Yranttay, isyay unfityay otay ebay ethay ulerray 
ofyay ayay eefray Eoplepay. 
 
Ornay avehay Eway eenbay antingway inyay attentionyay otay ouryay Ittishbray ethrenbray. 
Eway avehay arnedway emthay omfray imetay otay imetay ofyay attemptsyay ybay eirthay 
egislaturelay otay extendyay anyay unwarrantableyay urisdictionjay overyay usyay. 
Eway avehay emindedray emthay ofyay ethay ircumstancescay ofyay ouryay emigrationyay andyay 
ettlementsay erehay.  Eway avehay appealedyay otay eirthay ativenay usticejay 
andyay agnanimitymay, andyay eway avehay onjuredcay emthay ybay ethay iestay ofyay ouryay 
ommoncay indredkay otay isavowday esethay usurpationsyay, ichwhay ouldway inevitablyyay 
interruptyay ouryay onnectionscay andyay orrespondencecay.  Eythay ootay avehay eenbay 
eafday otay ethay oicevay ofyay usticejay andyay ofyay onsanguinitycay.  Eway ustmay, ereforethay, 
acquiesceyay inyay ethay ecessitynay, ichwhay enouncesday ouryay Eparationsay, andyay oldhay emthay, 
asyay eway oldhay ethay estray ofyay ankindmay, Enemiesyay inyay Arway, inyay Eacepay Iendsfray. 
 
Eway, ereforethay, ethay Epresentativesray ofyay ethay Unitedyay Atesstay ofyay Americayay, 
inyay Eneralgay Ongresscay, Assembledyay, appealingyay otay ethay Upremesay Udgejay ofyay 
ethay orldway orfay ethay ectituderay ofyay ouryay intentionsyay, oday, inyay ethay Amenay, 
andyay ybay ethay Authorityyay ofyay ethay oodgay Eoplepay ofyay esethay Oloniescay, 
olemnlysay ublishpay andyay eclareday, Atthay esethay Unitedyay Oloniescay areyay, 
andyay ofyay Ightray oughtyay otay ebay Eefray andyay Independentyay Atesstay; 
atthay eythay areyay Absolvedyay omfray allyay Allegianceyay otay ethay Itishbray Owncray, 
andyay atthay allyay oliticalpay onnectioncay etweenbay emthay andyay ethay Atestay 
ofyay Eatgray Itainbray, isyay andyay oughtyay otay ebay otallytay issolvedday; 
andyay atthay asyay Eefray andyay Independentyay Atesstay, eythay avehay ullfay Owerpay otay 
evylay Arway, oncludecay Eacepay, ontractcay Alliancesyay, establishyay Ommercecay, 
andyay otay oday allyay otheryay Actsyay andyay Ingsthay ichwhay Independentyay Atesstay aymay 
ofyay ightray oday.  Andyay orfay ethay upportsay ofyay isthay Eclarationday, ithway ayay irmfay 
elianceray onyay ethay Otectionpray ofyay Ivineday Ovidencepray, eway utuallymay edgeplay
